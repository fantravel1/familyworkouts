# Vacation & Hotel Room Workout

Don't let travel derail your fitness! This workout fits in any space‚Äîhotel rooms, vacation rentals, cruise ship cabins, or visiting family. All you need is a small floor space and your family!

## Why Workout on Vacation?

- **Beat the travel bloat**: Sitting in cars/planes + indulgent food = sluggish
- **Better sleep**: Exercise helps with jet lag
- **More energy**: Explore vacation spots without getting tired
- **Family bonding**: Start the day together
- **Feel good**: Vacation shouldn't mean feeling blah

## Space Requirements

**Minimum:** One person can lie down and spread arms
**Optimal:** Living room area of a suite
**Works in:** Hotel room beside the bed, balcony, grass area nearby

## Wake-Up Warm-Up (3 minutes)

### Shake Off the Sleep (or Jet Lag!)

**Travel Stretches** (1 minute)
- Neck rolls (airplane stiffness!)
- Shoulder shrugs
- Arm circles
- Side bends
- Hip circles
- All those cramped muscles!

**Marching in Place** (1 minute)
- Easy march
- Arms swinging
- Wake up the body
- No jumping (quiet start)

**Gentle Twists** (1 minute)
- Feet planted
- Twist left and right
- Arms follow
- Loosening up!

## The Hotel Room Circuit (12 minutes)

### 3 Rounds - Quiet Version Available!

#### Exercise 1: Squats (45 seconds)
**Standard:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Squat deep
- Stand up
- Repeat

**Quiet Version:**
- Slow, controlled squats
- No jumping
- Neighbors won't know!

*15 seconds rest*

#### Exercise 2: Push-Ups (45 seconds)
**Standard:**
- Full or modified push-ups
- Chest to floor
- Push up

**Small Space Tip:**
- Against the wall if no floor space
- On the bed edge works too!

*15 seconds rest*

#### Exercise 3: Lunges (45 seconds)
**Standard:**
- Alternating forward lunges
- Step out and back
- 10 each leg

**Tiny Room Version:**
- Stationary split squats
- Don't step, just drop knee
- Same muscles, less space

*15 seconds rest*

#### Exercise 4: Plank (45 seconds)
**Standard:**
- Hold plank position
- Core engaged
- Perfect for quiet workout!

**Modifications:**
- Knees down
- Forearms or hands
- Whatever fits your space

*15 seconds rest*

#### Exercise 5: Mountain Climbers (45 seconds)
**Standard:**
- Plank position
- Drive knees to chest
- Alternating, fast

**Quiet Version:**
- Slow, controlled "stepping" climbers
- One foot at a time
- No thumping!

*15 seconds rest*

#### Exercise 6: Tricep Dips (45 seconds)
**Standard:**
- Hands on chair/bed edge
- Lower and push up
- Triceps working!

**Hotel Hack:**
- Desk chair works great
- Edge of bed
- Sturdy suitcase (packed!)

*15 seconds rest - then repeat circuit!*

## Vacation Cardio (5 minutes)

### Get That Heart Rate Up!

**Option A: Ground Floor or Outside**
If you can be loud:

**Jumping Jacks** (1 minute)
- Full range of motion
- Arms and legs moving
- Classic cardio

**High Knees** (1 minute)
- Run in place
- Knees up high
- Arms pumping

**Burpees** (1 minute)
- Full burpee
- Down, push-up, jump up
- The vacation calorie burner!

**Speed Skaters** (1 minute)
- Leap side to side
- Land on one foot
- Athletic movement

**Jump Squats** (1 minute)
- Squat and explode
- Soft landings
- Power move!

---

**Option B: Upper Floor or Quiet Needed**
Neighbor-friendly version:

**Step Touches** (1 minute)
- Side to side
- Arms moving
- Quick feet, no impact

**High Knee March** (1 minute)
- Marching not running
- Knees up
- Arms moving

**Squats with Calf Raise** (1 minute)
- Squat down
- Come up onto toes
- Controlled, quiet

**Power Walking in Place** (1 minute)
- FAST walking motion
- Arms really swing
- Speed without jumping

**Silent Burpees** (1 minute)
- Step back to plank (no jump)
- Lower body
- Step forward (no jump)
- Stand and reach
- Challenging but quiet!

## Quick Cool-Down (2 minutes)

### Stretch It Out

**Forward Fold** (20 seconds)
- Bend forward, let arms hang
- Stretch hamstrings

**Quad Stretch** (20 seconds each leg)
- Stand on one foot
- Pull other foot to glute
- Hold chair or wall

**Chest Opener** (20 seconds)
- Hands behind back
- Open up chest
- Deep breath

**Neck Stretch** (20 seconds)
- Ear to shoulder
- Hold, switch sides
- Release tension

**Deep Breathing** (20 seconds)
- Stand tall
- 3 deep breaths
- Ready for vacation!

- Walking counts
- Playing counts

### It's Still Vacation!
- Don't overdo it

- Some days, skip it
- Explore actively instead
- Rest is part of fitness

### Signs to Take a Day Off
- Still tired from yesterday
- Sore muscles
- Vacation is packed with activity
- You just need a break
- That's okay!
