Turn your neighborhood walk into an exciting fitness adventure! Find nature items, complete exercises, and discover the world around you.

## Why This Works

- **Exploration motivation**: The hunt keeps you moving
- **Unpredictable path**: No two workouts are the same
- **Nature connection**: Mindfulness meets movement
- **All ages included**: Everyone can find something
- **Self-paced**: Natural interval training

## What You'll Need

- Comfortable walking shoes
- Water bottle
- This checklist (screenshot or print it!)
- Optional: Bag for collecting (leave living things!)
- Sense of adventure!

---

## The Rules

1. **Find an item** from the list below
2. **Complete the exercise** assigned to that item
3. **Check it off** and move to the next
4. **Keep moving** between finds (walk/jog)
5. **Complete as many as possible** in 45 minutes!

## Movement Between Finds

While searching, keep moving!

**Level 1: Walking**
- Brisk pace
- Arms swinging

**Level 2: Power Walking**
- Fast walk
- Exaggerated arm movements

**Level 3: Jog Intervals**
- Jog 30 seconds
- Walk 30 seconds
- Repeat

**Level 4: Run**
- Continuous jogging
- Sprint to each find!

## Family Variations

### Team Scavenger
- Divide into teams
- Same list, different directions
- Meet back at start
- Most finds + exercises = winner!

### Relay Style
- One person searches
- When they find something, tag the next person
- That person does the exercise
- Switch roles

### Points Version
- Easy finds = 1 point
- Medium finds = 2 points
- Hard finds = 3 points
- Bonus finds = 5 points
- Most points wins!

### Photo Hunt
- Must take photo of each find
- Create a nature album
- Share on family chat

## Safety Reminders

1. **Stay aware**: Watch for traffic, obstacles
2. **Sun protection**: Hat, sunscreen
3. **Hydrate**: Bring water!
4. **Stay together**: Kids stay with adults
5. **Don't touch**: Some plants/animals aren't friendly
6. **Tell someone**: Let others know your route
7. **Phone charged**: For emergencies and photos!
