## Superhero Breathing Techniques

### Power Breath
- Breathe in for 4 counts (gathering power)
- Hold for 4 counts (building strength)
- Breathe out for 4 counts (releasing power)
- Use before hard poses!

### Calm Hero Breath
- Breathe in slowly (peace in)
- Breathe out slowly (worry out)
- Use during rest poses

### Energize Breath
- Quick breaths in and out through nose
- 10 quick breaths
- Then one big breath and hold
- Release!
