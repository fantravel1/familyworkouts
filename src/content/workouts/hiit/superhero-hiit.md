## Warm-Up: Hero Activation (3 minutes)

### Power-Up Sequence

**Hero March (30 seconds)**
- March in place with purpose
- Arms pumping like you mean it
- Head high, eyes forward

**Arm Blasters (30 seconds)**
- Punch forward alternating
- Fast and controlled
- Channel your inner hero

**Power Squats (30 seconds)**
- Slow, controlled squats
- Arms up like you're lifting a car
- Feel that hero strength!

**Hero Shuffle (30 seconds)**
- Side shuffle left and right
- Stay low, stay ready
- Quick feet, quick hero

**Super Stretch (60 seconds)**
- Reach for the sky (30 sec)
- Touch the ground (15 sec)
- Twist side to side (15 sec)

## HIIT Round 1: Marvel Heroes (5 minutes)

*30 seconds work / 15 seconds rest per exercise*

### 1. Spider-Man Wall Climbers
- Mountain climber position
- Bring knees toward elbows (wide)
- Like climbing up a building
- Fast and agile!

### 2. Iron Man Thrusters
- Squat down
- Explode up with arms overhead
- "Blast off" with each rep
- Land softly, repeat

### 3. Hulk Smash Burpees
- Full burpee
- At the top, "smash" fists together overhead
- ROAR optional but encouraged!
- Powerful movements

### 4. Thor Hammer Swings
- Wide stance
- Clasp hands together
- Swing "hammer" side to side
- Engage your core

### 5. Black Widow Quick Kicks
- Alternating front kicks
- Fast and controlled
- Hands up in fighting stance
- Strike with precision

### 6. Captain America Shield Throws
- Lateral lunge right, reaching arm
- Quickly switch to left
- Like throwing a shield
- Low and powerful

**60-second rest, hydrate!**

## HIIT Round 2: DC Heroes (5 minutes)

*30 seconds work / 15 seconds rest per exercise*

### 1. Superman Flyers
- Lie face down
- Lift arms, chest, and legs
- "Fly" by extending fully
- Hold briefly at top

### 2. Batman Box Jumps
- Use imaginary box (or step)
- Jump up and land soft
- Dark knight stealth landings
- No step? Squat jumps work!

### 3. Wonder Woman Warrior Bursts
- Warrior II position
- Pulse in lunge
- Quick switch sides
- Powerful and graceful

### 4. Flash Speed Feet
- High knees at MAXIMUM speed
- As fast as you can go!
- You ARE the Flash
- Channel that speed force

### 5. Aquaman Swimmers
- Lie face down
- Flutter arms and legs
- Swimming through the ocean
- Keep core engaged

### 6. Green Lantern Ring Circles
- Arms out to sides
- Small circles forward
- Small circles backward
- Your willpower creates the workout!

**60-second rest, hydrate!**

## HIIT Round 3: Team Training (5 minutes)

*All heroes unite! 40 seconds work / 20 seconds rest*

### 1. Avengers Assemble
- Star jump burpee
- Jump up with arms and legs wide
- "ASSEMBLE!" at the top
- Land and repeat

### 2. Justice League Power-Ups
- Squat hold
- Every 5 seconds: jump up
- Yell a hero name each jump
- "SUPERMAN!" "BATMAN!" etc.

### 3. X-Men Mutation
- Start in squat
- "Mutate" into different animal walks:
  - 10 sec bear crawl
  - 10 sec crab walk
  - 10 sec frog jumps
  - 10 sec gorilla shuffle

### 4. Guardians of the Galaxy Space Jump
- Jump forward 3 times
- Turn 180 degrees
- Jump forward 3 times back
- Protect the galaxy!

### 5. Hero Finale: Ultimate Power
- 10 jumping jacks
- 10 punches
- 10 kicks
- 10 squats
- NO REST - push through!

## Cool-Down: Hero Recovery (3 minutes)

Even heroes need to recover!

### Super Stretch Sequence

**Deep Breathing (30 seconds)**
- Hands on heart
- Slow inhale through nose
- Long exhale through mouth
- Feel your heart rate lower

**Quad Pull (30 seconds each leg)**
- Stand on one leg
- Pull foot to glutes
- Hero balance challenge!

**Forward Fold (30 seconds)**
- Bend forward from hips
- Let arms hang heavy
- Shake head yes and no

**Hero's Rest Pose (30 seconds)**
- Child's pose position
- Arms extended forward
- Breathe and recover

**Final Power Pose (30 seconds)**
- Stand tall
- Hands on hips
- You ARE a superhero
- Feel that power!

## Difficulty Levels

### Sidekick Mode (Easier)
- 20 seconds work / 20 seconds rest
- Skip burpees (do squats instead)
- Slower pace on all moves
- More rest between rounds

### Hero Mode (Standard)
- 30 seconds work / 15 seconds rest
- Full exercises as written
- Challenge yourself!
- Beat your previous effort

### Avenger Mode (Advanced)
- 40 seconds work / 10 seconds rest
- Add weights if available
- Higher jumps, faster moves
- Minimal rest between rounds

### Villain Mode (Extreme)
- 45 seconds work / 5 seconds rest
- Double the rounds
- Add explosive elements
- Only for true heroes!

### Villain Chase

3. Finish early = villain defeated!
4. Track family victories

### Hero Creation
