# 5-Minute Energy Burst

Only have 5 minutes? That's ALL you need! This quick burst workout delivers maximum energy and results in minimum time.

## Why 5 Minutes Works

- **Something beats nothing**: Consistency trumps duration
- **Manageable commitment**: Anyone can find 5 minutes
- **High intensity possible**: Short duration = can go ALL OUT
- **Energy boost**: Quick workout = immediate energy lift
- **Habit building**: Easy to make a daily habit

## The Formula

```
30 seconds WORK (maximum effort!)
10 seconds TRANSITION
Ã— 8 exercises
= 5 minutes 20 seconds
```

## Quick Warm-Up (40 seconds)

### Speed Activation

**Arm Swings** (10 seconds)
- Big swings forward and back
- Get blood flowing

**Hip Circles** (10 seconds)
- Quick circles
- Both directions

**High Knee March** (10 seconds)
- Quick marching
- Arms pumping

**Jumping Prep** (10 seconds)
- Small bounces
- Ready to explode!

## THE 5-MINUTE BLAST

### Exercise 1: Jumping Jacks (30 sec)
**GO ALL OUT!**
- Full jumping jacks
- Arms all the way up
- Legs wide apart
- MAXIMUM speed!

*10 second transition*

### Exercise 2: Squats (30 sec)
**SIT AND STAND FAST!**
- Deep squats
- Touch something at bottom if needed
- Fast but controlled
- Thighs burn = working!

*10 second transition*

### Exercise 3: High Knees (30 sec)
**PUMP THOSE KNEES!**
- Knees to waist height
- Arms pumping
- As fast as you can
- Sprint intensity!

*10 second transition*

### Exercise 4: Push-Ups (30 sec)
**CHEST TO FLOOR!**
- Any variation works
- Wall, knee, or full
- Maximum reps
- Quality counts!

*10 second transition*

### Exercise 5: Mountain Climbers (30 sec)
**CLIMB THAT MOUNTAIN!**
- Plank position
- Knees drive to chest
- Alternating fast
- Don't stop!

*10 second transition*

### Exercise 6: Lunges (30 sec)
**STEP AND DIP!**
- Alternating lunges
- Deep knee bend
- Powerful push back
- Keep moving!

*10 second transition*

### Exercise 7: Burpees (30 sec)
**THE ULTIMATE MOVE!**
- Down to floor
- Chest touches
- Jump up
- As many as possible!

*10 second transition*

### Exercise 8: Plank Hold (30 sec)
**HOLD STRONG!**
- Forearms or hands
- Body straight
- Don't let hips drop
- FINISH STRONG!

## DONE! Time: 5:20

## Quick Cool-Down (Optional +40 seconds)

If you have an extra minute:

**Deep Breaths** (20 seconds)
- Hands on knees
- Slow inhales
- Long exhales
- Heart rate lowering

**Quick Stretch** (20 seconds)
- Reach up high
- Fold forward
- One deep breath
- Ready for your day!
