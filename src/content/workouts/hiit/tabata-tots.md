# Tabata Tots: 4-Minute Family Bursts

## Why Tabata Works for Families

- **Only 4 minutes per round**: Short attention spans? No problem!
- **Built-in rest**: 10-second breaks prevent burnout
- **Measurable progress**: Count reps, beat your record
- **Stackable**: Do 1 round or 5 based on energy
- **No equipment**: Start instantly anywhere

## Warm-Up Wiggle (2 minutes)

### Quick Body Wake-Up

**Shake It Out (30 seconds)**
- Shake hands
- Shake feet
- Shake whole body
- Shake out the sleepies!

**March & Clap (30 seconds)**
- March in place
- Clap on each step
- Get rhythm going

**Arm Swings (30 seconds)**
- Big arm circles
- Swing arms front to back
- Loosen up those shoulders

**Squat & Reach (30 seconds)**
- Gentle squat down
- Reach arms up
- Wake up those legs!

## Age-Specific Modifications

### For Toddlers (2-3 years)
- Use only 1-2 Tabata rounds
- Simpler movements (march, jump, stomp)
- Parent demonstrates everything
- No expectation of perfect form
- Celebrate ALL movement!

### For Preschoolers (3-5 years)
- 2-3 Tabata rounds
- Add animal themes
- Let them pick some exercises
- Counting games during rest
- Stickers for completing rounds

### For Big Kids (6+)
- Full 4+ Tabata rounds
- More complex moves
- Track their rep counts
- Challenge them to beat scores
- Can lead rounds for younger siblings

### For Adults (Doing it with kids)
- Model proper form
- Add difficulty to your moves
- Encourage without critiquing
- Match energy to kids
- Have fun first, form second!

## Making It a Game

### Rep Counting Challenge
During each 20-second interval:
- Count how many reps you do
- Write it down (parent helps)
- Try to beat it next time!
- Family leaderboard

### Mystery Movement
- Write exercises on paper slips
- Draw one for each round
- Surprise keeps it exciting!
- Kids can add their own ideas

## Cool-Down Cuddles (2 minutes)

### Gentle Finish

**Slow Walking (30 seconds)**
- Walk slowly around room
- Deep breaths
- Let heart rate lower

**Stretchy Cat (30 seconds)**
- On hands and knees
- Arch back up (cat)
- Dip belly down (cow)
- Gentle and slow

**Butterfly Wings (30 seconds)**
- Sit with feet together
- Knees out like butterfly
- Gently flap "wings"

**Sleepy Star (30 seconds)**
- Lie on back
- Arms and legs spread
- Deep breaths
- Rest and relax

## Parent Tips

1. **Lower expectations**: Participation = success
2. **Join in fully**: Kids copy enthusiastic parents
3. **Keep it silly**: Laughter matters more than form
4. **Stay flexible**: Skip exercises that aren't working
5. **End happy**: Always finish on a win
6. **Consistency > intensity**: Short daily beats long occasional
