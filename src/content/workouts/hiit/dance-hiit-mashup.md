# Dance HIIT Mashup

Why choose between dancing and HIIT when you can have both? This workout combines sick beats with intense intervals for a workout that flies by!

## The Concept

- **Dance moves** during high-intensity intervals
- **Active recovery** with easier grooves
- **Music-driven** pacing
- **Zero boredom** guaranteed

## Warm-Up: Party Prep (3 minutes)

### Get the Body Ready

**March & Groove** (1 minute)
- March in place
- Add arm swings
- Add shoulder rolls
- Start nodding to the beat

**Side Steps** (1 minute)
- Step side to side
- Add hip movements
- Add arm reaches
- Feel the rhythm!

**Body Rolls & Shakes** (1 minute)
- Roll shoulders
- Roll hips
- Shake arms loose
- Shake legs loose
- Full body shimmy!

## HIIT Round 1: Pop Dance Explosion (4 minutes)

*40 seconds HIGH / 20 seconds GROOVE*

### Interval 1: Jump & Pump
**HIGH INTENSITY:**
- Jump in place
- Pump arms overhead
- Fast feet, high energy
- Like you're at a concert!

**RECOVERY:**
- Sway side to side
- Arms flow naturally
- Catch your breath
- Keep moving!

### Interval 2: Disco Points
**HIGH INTENSITY:**
- Point up, point down
- Add jump on each point
- Fast arm switches
- Saturday Night Fever style!

**RECOVERY:**
- Slow disco sway
- Rolling shoulders
- Easy footwork

### Interval 3: Running Man
**HIGH INTENSITY:**
- Classic running man move
- Arms pumping
- As fast as you can
- Don't stop moving!

**RECOVERY:**
- Slow motion running man
- Exaggerated movements
- Catch breath

### Interval 4: Star Jumps
**HIGH INTENSITY:**
- Jump into star shape
- Arms and legs wide
- Land and repeat
- BIG movements!

**RECOVERY:**
- Gentle bounce
- Arms relaxed
- Small movements

## HIIT Round 2: Hip-Hop Heat (4 minutes)

*40 seconds HIGH / 20 seconds GROOVE*

### Interval 1: Body Rolls & Squats
**HIGH INTENSITY:**

- Body roll up

### Interval 2: Speed Grapevine
**HIGH INTENSITY:**

- Grapevine left
- Keep it moving!

**RECOVERY:**

- Easy pace
- Flowing movement

### Interval 3: Bounce & Cross
**HIGH INTENSITY:**
- Bounce in place
- Cross arms in front
- Open wide
- Speed increases!

**RECOVERY:**
- Gentle bounce
- Arms relaxed
- Finding breath

### Interval 4: Crazy Legs
**HIGH INTENSITY:**
- Kicks forward, back, side
- Arms pumping
- Non-stop leg movement
- Go wild!

**RECOVERY:**
- Slow kicks
- Controlled movement
- Balance focus

## Cool-Down: Groove Down (3 minutes)

### Slow Jam Stretches

**Swaying Stretches** (1 minute)
- Sway side to side
- Arms reaching up on each side
- Deep breaths
- Heart rate lowering

**Hip Circles** (30 seconds)
- Slow, sensual circles
- Both directions
- Release hip tension

**Shoulder Rolls** (30 seconds)
- Big, slow rolls
- Forward and back
- Let tension go

**Neck Stretches** (30 seconds)
- Slow head tilts
- Ear to shoulder
- Both sides
- Gentle movements

**Final Dance Stretch** (30 seconds)
- Arms overhead
- Side stretch left
- Side stretch right
- Forward fold
- Slow rise

## Intensity Levels

### Level 1: Party Starter
- 30 seconds work / 30 seconds rest
- Lower impact moves
- Focus on groove
- Perfect for beginners

### Level 2: Dance Floor Regular
- 40 seconds work / 20 seconds rest
- Standard intensity
- As written above
- Good challenge

### Level 3: Dance Machine
- 45 seconds work / 15 seconds rest
- Add jumps to everything
- Maximum output
- For serious dancers!

### Level 4: Dance Maniac
- 50 seconds work / 10 seconds rest
- Highest intensity
- No breaks
- Elite level!
