## Why This Works

- **Zero equipment cost**: Use what you have
- **No excuses**: It's always there

## Safety First!

Before we start:
- **Test stability** of any furniture before using
- **Clear the area** of breakable items
- **Barefoot or grippy socks** recommended
- **Carpet or mat** under you for floor work
- **Adults supervise** kids with furniture exercises

## Warm-Up: Tour the Gym (4 minutes)

Walk around touching each "station":
- Touch the wall: 10 wall push-ups
- Touch the couch: 10 incline push-ups
- Touch the chair: 10 step-ups (5 each leg)
- Touch the floor: 10 squats
- Repeat circuit!

---

## The Workout

### Station 1: Chair Exercises (5 minutes)

**Chair Dips**
1. Hands on chair seat edge
2. Feet out in front
3. Lower body by bending elbows
4. Push back up
5. 10 reps √ó 3 sets

*Easier: Knees bent, feet closer*
*Harder: Legs straight, feet on another chair*

**Chair Step-Ups**
1. Face the chair
2. Step up with right foot
3. Bring left foot up
4. Step down with right, then left
5. 10 each leg √ó 2 sets

*Easier: Lower surface*
*Harder: Hold weights, go faster*

**Chair Squats**
1. Stand in front of chair
2. Squat down until you touch the seat
3. Stand back up
4. DON'T sit‚Äîjust touch!
5. 15 reps √ó 2 sets

---

### Station 2: Wall Exercises (5 minutes)

**Wall Sit**
1. Back against wall
2. Slide down to chair position
3. Thighs parallel to floor
4. HOLD!
5. 30 seconds √ó 3 sets

*Challenge: Hold a pillow between knees*

**Wall Push-Ups**
1. Hands on wall, shoulder-width
2. Lean in, chest toward wall
3. Push back out
4. 15 reps √ó 2 sets

*Easier: Stand closer*
*Harder: Hands lower on wall*

**Wall Angels**
1. Back flat against wall
2. Arms up like goalposts
3. Slide arms up and down
4. Keep contact with wall
5. 15 reps √ó 2 sets

---

### Station 3: Couch Exercises (5 minutes)

**Couch Decline Push-Ups**
1. Hands on floor
2. Feet elevated on couch
3. Do push-ups
4. 8 reps √ó 3 sets

*Easier: Regular push-ups*
*Harder: Add a clap at bottom*

**Couch Hip Thrusts**
1. Upper back on couch edge
2. Feet flat on floor
3. Drop hips down
4. Drive up, squeeze glutes at top
5. 15 reps √ó 2 sets

*Add: Pillow between knees*

**Couch Bulgarian Split Squats**
1. One foot back on couch
2. Other foot forward
3. Lower into deep lunge
4. Drive up
5. 10 each leg √ó 2 sets

---

### Station 4: Floor Exercises with Props (5 minutes)

**Pillow Squeeze Bridges**
1. Lie on back, knees bent
2. Pillow between knees
3. Lift hips while squeezing pillow
4. Lower slowly
5. 15 reps √ó 2 sets

**Broom Overhead Squats**
1. Hold broom overhead, wide grip
2. Keep arms straight during squat
3. Squat deep
4. 10 reps √ó 3 sets

**Laundry Basket Deadlifts**
(Load basket with books or cans)
1. Basket on floor
2. Hip hinge to grab handles
3. Drive through heels to stand
4. Lower with control
5. 12 reps √ó 2 sets

---

### Station 5: Creative Combo Circuit (6 minutes)

**Do this circuit 3 times:**

1. **10 Chair Step-Ups** (alternating)
2. **10 Couch Push-Ups**
3. **20-second Wall Sit**
4. **10 Broom Twists** (Russian twist style)
5. **10 Pillow Squeezes** (hold pillow between knees, squeeze)
6. **30-second Plank**

*Rest 30 seconds between circuits*

## Cool-Down: Furniture Stretches (5 minutes)

**Chair Hip Flexor Stretch**
- One foot on chair
- Lunge forward
- 30 seconds each side

**Wall Calf Stretch**
- Hands on wall
- One foot back, heel down
- 30 seconds each side

**Couch Quad Stretch**
- Stand facing away from couch
- One foot back on couch
- Sink into stretch
- 30 seconds each side

**Floor Child's Pose**
- Kneel, reach forward
- Rest and breathe
- 1 minute

- Each family member invents an exercise using furniture
- Everyone tries it

### Hold the Fort
- Family wall sit contest

## Level Progressions

### Beginner
- Wall push-ups (not floor)
- Chair squats (sit all the way)
- High wall sits
- Assisted step-ups

### Intermediate
- Floor push-ups (with couch feet elevation)
- Touch-and-go chair squats
- 90-degree wall sits
- Step-ups with weights

### Advanced
- Decline push-ups with clap
- Single-leg chair squats
- Wall sits with weight
- Explosive jump step-ups
