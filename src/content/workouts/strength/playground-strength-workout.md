## Warm-Up: Playground Jog (4 minutes)

### Active Warm-Up Circuit
1. Jog around the playground perimeter (1 min)
2. High knees across the play area (30 sec)
3. Butt kicks back (30 sec)
4. Lateral shuffles along the fence (30 sec)
5. Arm circles (30 sec)
6. Leg swings on fence (30 sec)

---

## Station 1: Monkey Bars (5 minutes)

### Dead Hang
*Building grip strength*
- Grab bar, hang with straight arms
- Hold as long as possible
- Rest and repeat
- 3 rounds

**Goal by age:**
- Kids: 10-20 seconds
- Teens: 20-40 seconds
- Adults: 30-60+ seconds

### Monkey Bar Travel
*Upper body and grip*
- Start at one end
- Swing hand to hand across
- Turn around and come back
- Skip bars for challenge!

### Hanging Knee Raises
*Core on the bars*
- Hang from bar
- Bring knees to chest
- Lower with control
- 8-12 reps

### Pull-Up Progressions
*If able*
- Assisted: Jump up, lower slowly (negatives)
- Standard: Full pull-ups
- Challenge: Wide grip, commando grip
- 5-10 reps

---

## Station 2: Slide (5 minutes)

### Slide Bear Crawls
*Full body power*
- Start at bottom of slide
- Bear crawl UP the slide
- Slide down (the fun part!)
- 4-6 climbs

### Incline Push-Ups
*Chest and arms*
- Hands on slide edge, feet on ground
- Push-up position
- Lower chest to slide, push back up
- 10-15 reps

### Slide Plank Hold
*Core stability*
- Feet on slide, hands on ground
- Hold plank position
- Slide creates instability
- 30-45 second hold

### Slide Mountain Climbers
*Cardio + core*
- Plank with feet on slide
- Drive knees to chest
- The slide makes it HARDER
- 30 seconds

---

## Station 3: Swings (5 minutes)

### Swing Pike Push-Ups
*Shoulders and triceps*
- Feet in swing seat
- Hands on ground
- Pike position (hips high)
- Bend elbows, head toward ground
- 8-12 reps

### Swing Knee Tucks
*Core crusher*
- Plank with feet in swing
- Pull knees to chest
- Extend back out
- 10-15 reps

### Swing Rows
*Back and biceps*
- Hold swing chains
- Lean back at angle
- Pull chest to swing
- Lower with control
- 10-15 reps

### Swing Plank
*Core stability*
- Plank with feet in swing
- Just HOLD stable
- Swing wants to move you!
- 30-45 seconds

---

## Station 4: Benches (5 minutes)

### Step-Ups
*Leg strength*
- Step up with right foot
- Bring left foot up
- Step down
- Alternate lead foot
- 10 each leg

### Bench Dips
*Triceps*
- Hands on bench behind you
- Feet out in front
- Lower body by bending elbows
- Push back up
- 12-15 reps

### Elevated Push-Ups (Decline)
*Chest and shoulders*
- Feet on bench, hands on ground
- Push-up position
- Lower and push
- 10-12 reps

### Bulgarian Split Squats
*Single-leg strength*
- One foot back on bench
- Front foot forward
- Lower into lunge
- 8-10 each leg

---

## Station 5: Open Space (3 minutes)

### Broad Jumps
*Explosive power*
- Stand, swing arms back
- Jump forward as far as possible
- Land softly
- Walk back, repeat
- 6-8 jumps

### Bear Crawls
*Full body*
- On hands and feet
- Crawl forward and back
- Stay low!
- 30 seconds each direction

### Crab Walks
*Triceps and core*
- Belly up, hands and feet on ground
- Walk forward, backward, sideways
- 30 seconds

## Boss Level: The Playground Circuit (3 minutes)

**One time through‚Äîno rest!**

1. 5 Pull-ups (or dead hangs)
2. Run to slide: 3 bear crawls up
3. 10 Swing knee tucks
4. Run to bench: 10 step-ups
5. 10 Bench dips
6. Run back to start: 5 Broad jumps

## Cool-Down: Stretch & Slide (3 minutes)

### Post-Workout Stretches

**Shoulder Stretch**
- Use the pole: One arm across, lean in
- 30 seconds each

**Hip Flexor Stretch**
- One foot up on low platform
- Lean forward
- 30 seconds each

**Calf Stretch**
- Foot against slide base
- Lean in
- 30 seconds each

**Hang and Decompress**
- Dead hang on monkey bars
- Let spine lengthen
- 30 seconds

## Age Adaptations

### Young Kids (5-8)
- More play, less structure
- Monkey bar traversing = main goal
- Make it a game: "Don't touch the lava!"
- Shorter holds, more attempts

### Older Kids (9-12)
- Full workout structure
- Count reps, track progress
- Add challenges and competitions
- They can handle more difficulty

### Teens
- Add intensity
- Track personal records
- Create workout competitions
- Calisthenics skill work (muscle-ups!)

### Adults
- Full intensity bodyweight training
- Use smaller equipment (swing pike push-ups!)
- Early morning = empty playground
- Great functional fitness

---

## Safety at the Playground

### Rules
1. Check equipment before using (hot surfaces, wet, broken)
2. One person at a time on climbing equipment
3. Look before jumping off anything
4. No workout moves on equipment kids are actively playing on
5. Respect other families using the playground
6. Stay hydrated!

### Know Your Limits
- If a move feels unsafe, modify it
- Playgrounds are for play first, fitness second
- Don't interfere with kids' play
- Off-peak hours = better workout time
