## The Basic Salsa Rhythm

Salsa is danced in 8-count phrases:
- **Counts 1-2-3**: Step pattern
- **Count 4**: Pause (tap or hold)
- **Counts 5-6-7**: Step pattern
- **Count 8**: Pause (tap or hold)

"Quick-quick-slow, quick-quick-slow"

## Warm-Up: Feel the Beat (4 minutes)

### Salsa March
- March in place to salsa music
- Feel the rhythm in your body
- Add hip movement naturally
- 1 minute

### Shoulder Shimmy
- Alternate shoulders forward and back
- Keep it loose and playful
- Speed up with the music
- 30 seconds

### Hip Circles
- Hands on hips
- Circle hips right, then left
- Make them bigger!
- 1 minute

### Cuban Motion Practice
- Weight shifts side to side
- Let hips follow naturally
- This is the "sauce" in salsa!
- 1 minute

## Basic Steps (10 minutes)

### The Basic Step (Forward/Back)

**Leader's Basic:**
1. Step LEFT foot forward (count 1)
2. Weight back to RIGHT foot (count 2)
3. LEFT foot returns beside RIGHT (count 3)
4. Pause/tap (count 4)
5. Step RIGHT foot back (count 5)
6. Weight forward to LEFT foot (count 6)
7. RIGHT foot returns beside LEFT (count 7)
8. Pause/tap (count 8)

**Follower's Basic:**
- Mirror opposite: Back when leader goes forward

**Family Practice:**
- Everyone does leader's basic first
- Practice 2 minutes
- Then try with partners!

### Side Basic

1. Step LEFT to the side (count 1)
2. RIGHT foot joins LEFT (count 2)
3. Weight on LEFT (count 3)
4. Pause (count 4)
5. Step RIGHT to the side (count 5)
6. LEFT foot joins RIGHT (count 6)
7. Weight on RIGHT (count 7)
8. Pause (count 8)

**Practice 2 minutes**

### Adding Arms

- Arms bent at sides, elbows back
- Natural opposition: Left foot forward = right arm forward
- Keep arms relaxed, not stiff
- Let them swing with the movement

## Partner Work (6 minutes)

### Closed Position Hold
- Partners face each other
- Leader's right hand on follower's back
- Follower's left hand on leader's shoulder
- Free hands clasped at side, elbow height
- Maintain frame but stay relaxed

### Basic Step Together
- Leader does forward basic
- Follower mirrors (back basic)
- Stay connected through hands
- Eyes up, smile at partner!
- Practice 2 minutes

### Simple Turn (Right Turn)
- Leader raises left hand
- Follower turns right under the arm
- 4 counts to complete turn
- Return to basic step
- Practice 2 minutes

### Reverse Turn (Left Turn)
- Leader raises left hand
- Follower turns left under arm
- 4 counts to complete
- Practice both directions!

## Fun Combinations (3 minutes)

### "The Family Salsa"
Put it together:
1. 8 counts: Basic step
2. 8 counts: Side basic
3. 8 counts: Basic step
4. 8 counts: Right turn
5. Repeat!

### Circle Salsa
- Whole family in a circle
- Everyone does basic facing center
- On cue: Turn to face the person on your right
- 8 counts together
- Turn to face center again
- Rotate partners!
