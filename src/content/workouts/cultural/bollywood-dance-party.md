## Welcome to Bollywood!

Bollywood dance combines classical Indian dance forms with modern moves, creating an expressive, joyful style that's perfect for families. The emphasis on facial expressions and storytelling through movement makes it especially engaging for children.

## Why Bollywood Dance is Perfect for Families

- **Expressive**: Encourages showing emotions through movement
- **Joyful**: The music is uplifting and energizing
- **Inclusive**: No "wrong" way to express yourself
- **Cultural learning**: Introduction to Indian arts and traditions
- **Great cardio**: Fast-paced movements get hearts pumping

## Workout Breakdown

### Opening Celebration (3 minutes)

**Basic Bollywood Bounce**
- Feet hip-width apart
- Bounce knees to the beat
- Add shoulder shimmies
- Smile! Expression is everything in Bollywood

**Arm Patterns**
- Light bulb hands: twist wrists like screwing in bulbs
- Namaste position: palms together at heart
- Open arms wide: celebrate!
- Snake arms: wave arms fluidly

### Main Choreography (12 minutes)

**Move 1: The Classic Bollywood Step**
1. Step right, tap left foot behind
2. Arms sweep right
3. Step left, tap right foot behind
4. Arms sweep left
5. Add head movements (tilt side to side)

**Move 2: Bhangra Shoulders**
- Shoulder shrugs up and down
- Add bouncing knees
- Raise arms overhead and pulse
- Family challenge: Who has the bounciest shoulders?

**Move 3: Classical Hands (Mudras)**
- Lotus flower: fingers spread, wrists touching
- Peacock: thumb and forefinger touch
- Fish: palms together, wave like swimming
- Let kids create their own hand shapes!

**Move 4: The Bollywood Walk**
- Walk forward 4 counts with attitude
- Add hip sways
- Frame your face with hands
- Walk backward 4 counts
- Strike a pose!

**Move 5: Spinning Celebration**
- Arms out to sides
- Slow spin to the right (4 counts)
- Slow spin to the left (4 counts)
- Younger kids: Hold hands with parent and spin together

### Dance Routine: "Family Celebration" (5 minutes)

Put it all together:
1. 8 counts: Bollywood bounce with light bulb hands
2. 8 counts: Classic Bollywood step (right and left)
3. 8 counts: Bhangra shoulders with arms up
4. 8 counts: Bollywood walk forward and back
5. 4 counts: Spin!
6. 4 counts: Strike your best pose
7. Repeat!

## Family Expression Games

### "Emotion Dance"
- Call out emotions: happy, surprised, shy, proud
- Everyone dances expressing that emotion
- Bollywood is all about BIG expressions!

### "Mirror Dance"
- Partners face each other
- One person leads, other mirrors
- Switch leaders every 30 seconds
- Great for parent-child bonding

### "Freeze Dance Bollywood"
- Dance when music plays
- Freeze in a Bollywood pose when it stops
- Silliest pose wins!

## Age Adaptations

### Toddlers & Preschool (2-5)
- Focus on bouncing and spinning
- Use colorful scarves to wave
- Keep movements simple and repetitive
- Lots of praise for any movement!

### Kids (6-12)
- Add more complex arm patterns
- Introduce short choreography sequences
- Let them create their own "Bollywood move"
- Film a family music video!

### Teens
- Faster, more challenging choreography
- Explore different Bollywood eras and styles
- Learn about the film industry and culture
- Create TikTok-worthy routines

### Adults & Seniors
- Focus on expression over athleticism
- Chair Bollywood: All upper body movements
- Great for mood boosting and stress relief
