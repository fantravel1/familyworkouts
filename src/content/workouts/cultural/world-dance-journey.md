## Stop 1: Hawaii, USA - Hula (4 minutes)

### Aloha! üå∫

**About Hula:**
Hula tells stories through movement. Every hand gesture has meaning‚Äîocean waves, flowers, birds, and more.

**Basic Hula Step** (1.5 minutes)
1. Step right, bring left to meet (hips sway!)
2. Step left, bring right to meet
3. Knees slightly bent
4. Hips naturally sway
5. Smooth and flowing

**Storytelling Hands** (1.5 minutes)
Try these gestures:
- **Ocean waves**: Hands ripple side to side
- **Sun**: Arms rise in an arc
- **Rain**: Fingers flutter down
- **Love/Heart**: Hands cross over heart
- **Flower**: Fingers open like petals

**Hula Combination** (1 minute)
1. Step-touch right with "sun" arms
2. Step-touch left with "wave" hands
3. Sway in place with "rain" fingers
4. Hands to heart
5. Aloha!

**Hawaiian Word:** "Aloha" means hello, goodbye, and love!

**About Irish Step:**
Famous for quick footwork while keeping upper body still. Arms stay at sides‚Äîall the action is in the feet!

**Basic Step Pattern** (1.5 minutes)
1. Stand tall, arms at sides
2. Hop on right foot
3. Point left toe forward
4. Hop again, bring left foot back
5. Repeat other side
6. Quick, light feet!

**The Reel** (1.5 minutes)
1. Point, hop, point, hop
2. Three quick steps (1-2-3)
3. Repeat
4. Keep upper body STILL
5. Light as a feather!

**Irish Jump** (1 minute)
1. Jump with both feet
2. Click heels in the air if you can!
3. Land softly
4. Arms stay down
5. Proud and strong!

## Stop 3: West Africa - Afrobeat (4 minutes)

### Karibu! ü•Å

**About African Dance:**
Rooted in community and celebration. Full body involvement with grounded, powerful movements. Often done in circles.

**African Bounce** (1 minute)
1. Feet hip-width apart
2. Bend knees, bounce to beat
3. Whole body moves DOWN
4. Grounded and strong
5. Feel the earth!

**Shoulder Isolations** (1 minute)
1. One shoulder forward
2. Other shoulder forward
3. Alternate quickly
4. Add the bounce!
5. Upper body loose

**African Step Pattern** (1 minute)
1. Step right, stomp
2. Step left, stomp
3. Arms swing naturally
4. Power from the core
5. Celebrate!

**Circle Dance** (1 minute)
1. Form a family circle
2. Everyone bounces together
3. One person goes to center
4. Shows off moves
5. Next person!
6. Community energy!

**Swahili Word:** "Karibu" means "welcome!"

## Stop 4: India - Bollywood (4 minutes)

### Namaste! ü™∑

**About Bollywood:**
High energy, expressive, and colorful! Bollywood combines classical Indian dance with modern moves. Big expressions and hand gestures!

**The Bollywood Bounce** (1 minute)
1. Bounce in place
2. Change light bulb (hand twist overhead)
3. Screw in light bulb (hand twist to side)
4. Classic Bollywood move!

**Mudra Hands** (1 minute)
Try these hand positions:
- **Namaste**: Palms together at heart
- **Flower**: Fingers bloom open
- **Bird**: Hands flutter like wings
- **Eyes**: Fingers frame your eyes
- Expressive and beautiful!

**Hip Shimmy** (1 minute)
1. Hands on hips
2. Quick hip side to side
3. Add arm patterns
4. Big smile!
5. Joyful energy!

**Bollywood Combo** (1 minute)
1. Light bulb screw (4 counts)
2. Hip shimmy (4 counts)
3. Arms wide, step side (4 counts)
4. Namaste bow (4 counts)
5. Feel like a Bollywood star!

**Hindi Word:** "Namaste" means "I bow to you" (respectful greeting)!

## Stop 5: Brazil - Samba (4 minutes)

### Ol√°! üé≠

**About Samba:**
The heartbeat of Brazilian Carnival! Characterized by the quick hip movement and energetic footwork. Pure joy in motion!

**Basic Samba Step** (1.5 minutes)
1. Step right foot forward
2. Bring left to right
3. Step right foot back
4. The same with opposite feet
5. Quick 1-2-3 rhythm
6. Hips move naturally!

**Samba Bounce** (1 minute)
1. Slight bounce in knees
2. Hips rock side to side
3. Quick and light
4. Arms relaxed
5. Feel the Carnival!

**Samba Arms** (1 minute)
1. Arms swing forward and back
2. Opposite arm to leg
3. Like marching but dancier!
4. Add shoulder movement
5. Celebration energy!

**Carnival Parade** (30 seconds)
1. Put it all together
2. Step, bounce, arms swing
3. Big smile!
4. You're in the parade!

**Portuguese Word:** "Alegria" means "joy!"

## Stop 6: Japan - Bon Odori (3 minutes)

### Konnichiwa! üéå

**About Bon Odori:**
Traditional summer festival dance. Graceful, flowing movements often done in a circle around a tower. Simple, meditative, beautiful.

**Basic Bon Steps** (1 minute)
1. Step right, touch left
2. Step left, touch right
3. Smooth and gentle
4. No bouncing
5. Peaceful movement

**Fan Hands** (1 minute)
1. Pretend to hold a fan
2. Gentle wrist movements
3. Graceful arcs
4. Paint the air
5. Elegant and calm

**Circle Formation** (1 minute)
1. Family forms a circle
2. All move same direction
3. Simple step-touches
4. Hands make graceful patterns
5. Unity and peace

**Japanese Word:** "Obon" is a festival to honor ancestors!
