# Disco Fever Family Dance Party

Grab your imaginary platform shoes and get ready to boogie! Disco dancing is the ultimate feel-good cardio workout that spans generations.

## Why Disco?

- **Timeless fun**: Grandparents know it, kids love learning it
- **High-energy cardio**: 70s dancers were seriously fit!
- **Easy to learn**: Basic moves, big fun
- **Constant movement**: You never stop grooving
- **Pure joy**: Impossible not to smile

## Warm-Up: Disco Prep (4 minutes)

### The Pre-Party Stretch

**Neck Rolls** (30 seconds)
- Slow circles right
- Slow circles left
- Loosen up that disco neck!

**Shoulder Shimmy** (30 seconds)
- Roll shoulders back
- Roll shoulders forward
- Add a little shimmy shake!

**Hip Circles** (30 seconds)
- Hands on hips
- Big circles right
- Big circles left
- Warm up those disco hips!

**Arm Grooves** (30 seconds)
- Reach right arm up and down
- Reach left arm up and down
- Both arms swimming through air

**Disco Walk in Place** (2 minutes)
- March to the beat
- Add arm swings
- Point those fingers!
- Feel the groove coming!

## Disco Move 1: The Point (4 minutes)

*The iconic John Travolta move!*

### Basic Point

1. Stand with feet hip-width apart
2. Shift weight to left foot
3. Point right arm UP diagonally
4. Look at your pointing finger
5. Bring arm down, shift right
6. Point LEFT arm UP diagonally
7. Alternate with the beat!

### Point Variations

**Low Point**
- Point down to the opposite corner
- Creates a cool sweeping motion

**Double Point**
- Two quick points same direction
- Then switch sides
- "Point-point... point-point"

**Walking Point**
- Add step touches while pointing
- Step right, point left arm up
- Step left, point right arm up
- Travel across the floor!

### Practice Time
- 2 minutes basic point
- 1 minute with variations
- 1 minute walking point

## Disco Move 2: The Hustle (5 minutes)

*The official dance of disco!*

### Basic Line Dance Hustle

**The 4-Count:**
1. Step RIGHT to the side
2. Step LEFT behind right
3. Step RIGHT to the side
4. TOUCH left foot beside right

**Then Reverse:**
1. Step LEFT to the side
2. Step RIGHT behind left
3. Step LEFT to the side
4. TOUCH right foot beside left

### Add Arms
- Arms swing naturally
- Or add claps on the touch
- Or point during the steps

### Partner Hustle
1. Face your partner
2. Mirror their moves
3. Hold hands and step together
4. Spin away and back!

### Practice Time
- 3 minutes solo hustle
- 2 minutes partner/group hustle

## Disco Move 3: The Spinning Finger (3 minutes)

### One-Finger Spin

1. Point index finger up
2. Make small circles in the air
3. Move hand up and down
4. Switch hands
5. Add bouncing knees!

### Two-Finger Spin

1. Both hands pointing up
2. Circle both in same direction
3. Circle both in opposite directions
4. Lower them, raise them!

### Full Arm Circles

1. Extend arm fully
2. Big circles overhead
3. Like you're lassoing the disco ball!
4. Switch arms
5. Both arms together!

## Disco Move 4: The Bus Stop (4 minutes)

*Another classic line dance!*

### Basic Bus Stop Steps

**Part 1: The Grapevine**
1. Step RIGHT to side
2. Step LEFT behind right
3. Step RIGHT to side
4. KICK left foot forward

**Part 2: Reverse**
1. Step LEFT to side
2. Step RIGHT behind left
3. Step LEFT to side
4. KICK right foot forward

**Part 3: Walk Back**
1. Step back with right
2. Step back with left
3. Step back with right
4. TOUCH left beside right

**Part 4: Hip Bumps**
1. Bump hips right twice
2. Bump hips left twice
3. TURN quarter turn right
4. START AGAIN!

### Family Line Formation
- Stand in a line
- All face same direction
- Do the Bus Stop together
- The quarter turns are hilarious!

## Disco Move 5: The YMCA (3 minutes)

*Everyone's favorite!*

### The Letters

**Y:** Arms up in V shape
**M:** Hands touch above head, elbows out
**C:** Both arms curve to the left
**A:** Hands touch above head in point

### Full Sequence
1. Sing "Y-M-C-A!"
2. Form each letter with arms
3. March or bounce between letters
4. Feel free to be dramatic!

### Challenge Version
- Squat on each letter
- Jump on each letter
- Turn on each letter

## Disco Move 6: Night Fever Bounce (3 minutes)

### Basic Disco Bounce

1. Feet hip-width apart
2. Bend knees slightly
3. Bounce with the beat
4. Add shoulder movements
5. Keep it loose and groovy!

### Add Arms
- Pump arms at your sides
- Swimming arms in front
- Reaching up and pulling down
- Classic "wax on, wax off" motion

### Full Night Fever
1. Bounce in place (4 counts)
2. Point sequence (4 counts)
3. Bounce (4 counts)
4. Hustle steps (4 counts)
5. Repeat!

## Disco Freestyle (4 minutes)

### Put It All Together!

**Song 1 (2 minutes)**
Play "Stayin' Alive" and practice:
- Start with the walk/bounce
- Add pointing when chorus hits
- Hustle during verses
- Spinning fingers at will!

**Song 2 (2 minutes)**
Play "Night Fever" and freestyle:
- Mix all the moves
- Make up your own combinations
- Express yourself!
- Don't stop moving!

## Family Disco Games

### Mirror, Mirror Disco
1. Partners face each other
2. One leads with disco moves
3. Other mirrors exactly
4. Switch leaders each song

### Disco Freeze
1. Dance when music plays
2. FREEZE when music stops
3. Strike a disco pose
4. Best pose wins!

### Soul Train Line
1. Form two lines facing each other
2. Create an "aisle" between
3. Take turns dancing down the aisle
4. Everyone cheers!
5. Classic Soul Train style!

## Cool-Down: Slow Jam (4 minutes)

### Wind Down Moves

**Slow Grooves** (1 minute)
- Keep dancing but half speed
- Smaller movements
- Deep breaths
- Let heart rate lower

**Reach and Sway** (1 minute)
- Feet planted
- Arms reach up, sway left
- Arms reach up, sway right
- Gentle side stretches

**Disco Ball Stretch** (1 minute)
- Reach both arms up
- Make small circles (like a disco ball)
- Lower arms slowly
- Roll shoulders out

**Final Pose** (1 minute)
- Strike your best disco pose
- Hold 10 seconds
- Different pose
- Hold 10 seconds
- Final pose, deep breath
- DISCO ROYALTY!
