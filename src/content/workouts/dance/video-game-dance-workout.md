## Game Loading... (Warm-Up) - 3 minutes

### Controller Warm-Up
- Button mashing fingers (fast finger wiggles)
- Joystick wrists (wrist circles)
- Shoulder button shrugs
- *30 seconds*

### Loading Bar March
- March in place
- Arms pump like a loading bar filling up
- Start slow, get faster as the "bar fills"
- *1 minute*

### Character Select Stretches
- Arms overhead: "Stretchy character!"
- Forward fold: "Bendy character!"
- Wide stance: "Strong character!"
- Balance one foot: "Agile character!"
- *1 minute*

## LEVEL 1: Classic Games (4 minutes)

### üçÑ MARIO MOVES

**Super Jump**
- Squat down low
- JUMP up high
- Reach one arm up (getting the coin!)
- Land softly
- *8 jumps*

**Fireball Throw**
- Lunge right, throw right arm (fireball!)
- Lunge left, throw left arm (fireball!)
- Add a "pew pew!" sound
- *8 each side*

**Flagpole Slide**
- Arms overhead, stand tall
- Slowly "slide" down into squat
- Pop back up, celebrate!
- *4 slides*

### üëª PAC-MAN CHASE

**Waka Waka Run**
- High knees in place
- Chomp arms (hands open and close)
- FAST when "ghosts" chase!
- *30 seconds*

**Ghost Dodge**
- Side shuffle right (escaping ghost!)
- Side shuffle left (another ghost!)
- Quick direction changes
- *30 seconds*

**Floss**
- Swing hips right, arms left

**Orange Justice**
- Swing arms in circles
- Kick legs alternating

## Parent/Non-Gamer Guide

### Learning the Lingo
- **NPC**: Non-Player Character
- **HP**: Health Points
- **XP**: Experience Points
- **Boss**: Big challenging enemy
- **Emote**: Dance or gesture in game
- **Speed Run**: Completing something fast

### Why This Works
- Kids already love these moves from games
- Familiarity increases engagement
- Gaming metaphors make exercise feel achievable
- It's a language they speak fluently!

### Join the Game
- Ask kids to teach YOU the moves
- Role reversal builds confidence
- Learn together‚Äîbond over gaming AND fitness
