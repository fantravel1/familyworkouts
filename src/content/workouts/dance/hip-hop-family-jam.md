# Hip Hop Family Jam

Time to bring the street to your living room! Learn real hip hop fundamentals in a fun, family-friendly way. No experience neededâ€”just bring your attitude!

## The Hip Hop Spirit

Hip hop is about:
- **Self-expression**: Your unique style matters
- **Community**: We dance together
- **Creativity**: Make moves your own
- **Confidence**: Own your space
- **Fun**: Above all else!

## The Four Elements

Hip hop culture includes:
1. **DJing** (music)
2. **MCing** (rapping)
3. **Breaking** (dance)
4. **Graffiti** (art)

Today we focus on BREAKING (also called b-boying/b-girling)!

## Warm-Up: Getting Loose (4 minutes)

### Street Style Prep

**Bounce It Out** (1 minute)
- Stand with feet shoulder-width
- Bounce to the beat
- Knees soft, body relaxed
- Feel the rhythm!

**Shoulder Rocks** (30 seconds)
- Rock shoulders forward and back
- One at a time, then together
- Add some attitude!

**Head Nods** (30 seconds)
- Nod head to the beat
- Different directions
- Neck nice and loose

**Body Rolls** (1 minute)
- Roll from head down
- Chest, core, hips
- Smooth and controlled
- Like a wave!

**Arm Isolations** (1 minute)
- Move just your arms
- One at a time
- Hitting different poses
- Getting control!

## Foundation Moves (8 minutes)

### The Basics Every Dancer Needs

**The Two-Step** (2 minutes)
*The foundation of hip hop dance!*

1. Step right, bring left to meet it
2. Step left, bring right to meet it
3. Add a bounce with each step
4. Arms move naturally
5. Simple but ESSENTIAL!

**Family Practice:**
- Start slow
- Speed up as you get it
- Add your own flavor!

**The Bounce** (1 minute)
*Feel the music in your body!*

1. Feet planted
2. Bend knees to the beat
3. Let whole body absorb the rhythm
4. This is your home base!

**Rock Step** (2 minutes)
*Movement with power!*

1. Step forward with right foot
2. Rock your weight forward
3. Step back to center
4. Step back with left
5. Rock weight back
6. Return to center
7. Add arm movements!

**Groove Walk** (1.5 minutes)
*Walking with style!*

1. Walk forward with swagger
2. Add shoulder movement
3. Head moves with confidence
4. Own every step!

**The Prep** (1.5 minutes)
*Getting ready to break!*

1. Step touch side to side
2. Add arm swing across body
3. Build momentum
4. Ready for anything!

## Top Rock (6 minutes)

### Dancing on Your Feet

**Indian Step** (1.5 minutes)
*Classic top rock move!*

1. Cross right foot behind left
2. Uncross and step out
3. Cross left behind right
4. Uncross and step out
5. Arms swing opposite to legs
6. Keep the bounce!

**Kick It Out** (1.5 minutes)
*Adding some flair!*

1. Kick right leg out
2. Bring it back
3. Kick left leg out
4. Bring it back
5. Arms go opposite
6. Attitude matters!

**Crossovers** (1.5 minutes)
*Footwork gets fancy!*

1. Step right across left
2. Step left out wide
3. Step left across right
4. Step right out wide
5. Keep low, stay bouncy!

**Top Rock Combo** (1.5 minutes)
*Put it together!*

1. 4 counts of two-step
2. 4 counts of Indian step
3. 4 counts of kick out
4. 4 counts of your choice!
5. Repeat and feel it!

## Freezes (5 minutes)

### Signature Hip Hop Poses

**Baby Freeze** (1.5 minutes)
*Beginner freeze!*

1. Get on hands and knees
2. Put head down
3. Elbow goes into hip
4. Lift knees off ground
5. Hold that pose!
6. *Modification: Keep knees down*

**Chair Freeze** (1.5 minutes)
*Stylish seated pose!*

1. Sit on one hip
2. One hand on ground
3. Other arm up in the air
4. Legs positioned cool
5. FREEZE and hold!

**Pointing Freeze** (1 minute)
*Classic attitude freeze!*

1. Stand in strong pose
2. Point to the crowd
3. Other hand on hip or chest
4. Face says "I got this!"
5. FREEZE!

**Jump Freeze** (1 minute)
*Dynamic freeze entry!*

1. Jump in the air
2. Land in a pose
3. FREEZE completely
4. Hold for 3 counts
5. Repeat with different poses!

## Arm Waves and Isolations (4 minutes)

### Control Your Body

**Basic Arm Wave** (1.5 minutes)
*The classic wave!*

1. Start with fingers
2. Move to wrist
3. Flow to elbow
4. Continue to shoulder
5. Send it across chest
6. Down the other arm!
7. Practice until smooth

**Body Wave** (1.5 minutes)
*Full body flow!*

1. Start at head
2. Flow through chest
3. Down through core
4. Through hips
5. Down to knees
6. One smooth wave!

**Pop Hits** (1 minute)
*Sharp muscle contractions!*

1. Contract muscle quickly
2. Arms, chest, or neck
3. Sharp POP on the beat
4. Like you're hit by electricity!
5. Practice with music

## Hip Hop Combos (5 minutes)

### Putting It All Together

**Combo 1: The Basic Flow**
1. 4 counts: Two-step right
2. 4 counts: Two-step left
3. 4 counts: Rock front
4. 4 counts: Freeze pose!

**Combo 2: Top Rock Fire**
1. 4 counts: Indian step
2. 4 counts: Kick out (R, L)
3. 4 counts: Crossovers
4. 4 counts: Jump freeze!

**Combo 3: Wave Rider**
1. 4 counts: Arm wave right
2. 4 counts: Arm wave left
3. 4 counts: Body wave
4. 4 counts: Freeze pose!

**Family Cypher** (2 minutes)
*Take turns in the circle!*

1. Form a circle
2. One person enters center
3. Do 8 counts of moves
4. End with a freeze
5. Next person jumps in!
6. Cheer each other on!

## Cool-Down: Respect the Dance (3 minutes)

### Hip Hop Always Ends with Respect

**Slow Bounce** (1 minute)
- Gentle bouncing
- Breathing returning to normal
- Still feeling the rhythm
- Easy movement

**Stretch Circle** (1.5 minutes)
- Arms across body
- Neck rolls
- Hip circles
- Leg stretches

**Dap It Up** (30 seconds)
- Give everyone props
- Fist bumps
- "Good job!" to each person
- Hip hop is community!

## Practice Tips

### For Beginners
- Start with bounce and two-step
- Don't worry about looking cool yet
- Feel the music first
- Watch and learn from everyone

### For Kids
- Make it a game
- Focus on freezes (kids love those!)
- Let them create their own moves
- Praise effort over perfection

### For Teens
- They might know more than you!
- Let them lead sometimes
- Learn trending moves together
- Respect their knowledge

### For Adults
- Let loose!
- Don't overthink it
- Your kids won't judge you
- Have FUN

## Level Up Challenges

### Week 1
- Master the two-step
- Learn 2 freezes
- Practice 5 min daily

### Week 2
- Add top rock
- Arm waves smooth
- Create 1 combo

### Week 3
- Cypher comfortable
- All freezes solid
- 8-count choreography

### Week 4
- Family routine complete
- Battle ready!
- Performance time?

## The Rules of Hip Hop

1. **Respect the culture** - Know the history
2. **Be original** - Add your style
3. **Support your crew** - We rise together
4. **Practice, practice, practice** - Skills take time
5. **Have fun** - Always the priority!

## Family Dance Battle Ideas

### Freeze Battle
- Play music
- When it stops, everyone freezes
- Best freeze wins that round!

### Move Copycat
- One person does a move
- Everyone copies
- Add on, keep building!

### Genre Switch
- DJ (someone) changes music style
- Dancers must adapt
- From hip hop to slow to fast!
