## Castle Wake-Up Warm-Up (3 minutes)

### Morning in the Kingdom

**Aurora's Sleepy Stretch** (30 seconds)
- Big yawning stretch
- Arms reaching overhead
- Waking from a long sleep!

**Snow White's Animal Friends Walk** (1 minute)
- Walk in place
- Wave to the birds
- Say good morning to the forest!
- Cheerful energy!

**Jasmine's Magic Carpet Float** (30 seconds)
- Arms out for balance
- Gentle swaying
- Flying over Agrabah!

**Ariel's Under the Sea Swishes** (30 seconds)
- Side to side movement
- Swimming through water
- Swishing your tail!

**Pocahontas's Wind Stretch** (30 seconds)
- Arms out, feeling the wind
- Gentle spins
- Colors of the wind flowing around you

## Act 1: Frozen Power (5 minutes)

### Elsa & Anna Training

**Let It Go Arms** (1 minute)
*"Let it go, let it go!"*
- Dramatic arm movements
- Building ice castles
- Reach and release
- Feel the power!

**Ice Crystal Squats** (1 minute)
*Creating ice crystals!*
- Squat down
- Rise up with arms creating shapes
- Magical ice formations!
- 15 crystal squats

**Snowflake Spins** (45 seconds)
*Spinning like a snowflake!*
- Gentle controlled spins
- Arms out like a snowflake
- Magical and graceful

**Elsa's Power Pose** (30 seconds)
*Show your strength!*
- Stand tall and proud
- Arms raised in power
- "The cold never bothered me anyway!"

**Anna's Determination Run** (45 seconds)
*Running to save Elsa!*
- Run in place
- Through the snow!
- Never give up!
- Sister love energy!

**Olaf's Summer Dance** (1 minute)
*Dreaming of sunshine!*
- Happy, bouncy movements
- Bees that buzz, dandelions fuzz!
- Pure joy!

## Act 2: Moana's Ocean Adventure (4 minutes)

### Wayfinding Training

**How Far I'll Go Reaches** (1 minute)
*Reaching beyond the reef!*
- Stand at the ocean's edge
- Reach toward the horizon
- How far will YOU go?
- Stretch and reach!

**Te Fiti Lunges** (1 minute)
*Restoring the heart!*
- Walking lunges
- Carrying the heart (hands at chest)
- Moving with purpose
- 10 each leg

**Maui's Fishhook Swings** (45 seconds)
*You're welcome!*
- Arm swings in big arcs
- Like swinging a giant fishhook
- Powerful demigod energy!

**Ocean Wave Squats** (1 minute)
*The ocean chose you!*
- Squat down (diving under)
- Rise up (riding the wave)
- Flow with the water
- 12 wave squats

**Tamatoa's Shiny Dance** (15 seconds)
*SHINY!*
- Sparkly movements
- Admire yourself
- Quick dance break!

## Act 3: Mulan's Warrior Training (4 minutes)

### Training with the Troops

**Be a Man Drills** (1 minute)
*Let's get down to business!*
- March in place
- Military style
- Swift as a coursing river!
- Strong and steady

**Warrior Lunges** (45 seconds)
*With all the force of a great typhoon!*
- Deep lunges
- Arms in fighting position
- Strong and powerful!

**Staff Training Twists** (45 seconds)
*Training with weapons!*
- Torso twists with arms extended
- Like swinging a staff
- Core engagement!

**Great Wall Climbs** (45 seconds)
*Climbing to save China!*
- High knee runs
- Climbing motion with arms
- Determination!

**Dragon Kicks** (45 seconds)
*Mushu's fiery moves!*
- Front kicks
- Side kicks
- "Dishonor on your cow!"
- Fast and fierce!

## Act 4: More Princess Power (5 minutes)

### Around the Kingdom

**Rapunzel's Tower Workout** (1 minute)
*"When will my life begin?"*
- Jumping jacks (bored in tower)
- Hair flip motions (toss your head!)
- Painting reaches
- Energetic waiting!

**Merida's Archery** (1 minute)
*Brave's bow and arrow!*
- Archer pose
- Draw back the bow
- Release! (lunge forward)
- Hit the target!
- 8 each side

**Tiana's Beignet Flips** (1 minute)
*Working hard in the kitchen!*
- Squat (grab the pan)
- Jump (flip the beignet!)
- Catch and repeat!
- Hard work pays off!

**Belle's Library Ladder Climbs** (1 minute)
*So many books to explore!*
- High knee marches
- Reaching up for books
- Climbing that beautiful library ladder!

**Cinderella's Midnight Sprint** (1 minute)
*The clock is striking 12!*
- Sprint in place!
- Losing your slipper!
- Run before the magic ends!
- Leave a glass slipper behind (stop dramatically)!

## Royal Ball Finale (3 minutes)

### All Princesses Unite!

**Princess Promenade** (1 minute)
- Elegant walking
- Heads held high
- Royal wave to the crowd
- Graceful and proud

**Royal Ball Dance** (1 minute)
- Waltz steps if you know them
- Or free dancing like at the ball
- Spinning with partners
- Magical and beautiful!

**Crown Pose Holds** (1 minute)
- Arms up like wearing a crown
- Hold with grace
- You ARE royalty!
- 20-second holds

## Cool-Down: Happily Ever After (3 minutes)

### Sunset in the Kingdom

**Sleeping Beauty Rest** (1 minute)
- Lie down gently
- Like Aurora before the kiss
- Deep, peaceful breaths
- Magical sleep

**Tale As Old As Time Stretch** (1 minute)
- Gentle stretching
- Flowing movements
- Beauty and grace
- Side stretches, forward folds

**Wish Upon a Star** (1 minute)
- Stand and look up
- Arms reaching to your star
- Make a wish
- "When you wish upon a star..."
- Final deep breath
