*Play upbeat 80s music throughout!*

- Knees high!

- Forward, forward, forward, forward
- Back, back, back, back
- Add a "WOO!"

**Side Reaches** (16 counts each)
- Right arm reaches over, 4 times
- Left arm reaches over, 4 times
- Big stretch through the side body!

- Hands on hips
- Circle those hips!

- Arms straight out

## Main Workout: The 80s Routine (16 minutes)

### Move 1: Grapevine (4 minutes)

**Basic Grapevine Right:**
1. Step RIGHT to the right
2. Step LEFT behind right
3. Step RIGHT to the right
4. Tap LEFT foot, CLAP!

**Grapevine Left:**
- Same thing, starting LEFT

**Repeat and add arms:**
- First time: Hands on hips
- Second time: Arms pump at sides
- Third time: Arms reach high on tap
- Fourth time: Add a turn on the tap!

*"Step-behind-step-CLAP!"*

---

### Move 2: V-Step (3 minutes)

**Basic V-Step:**
1. Step RIGHT foot out/forward diagonally
2. Step LEFT foot out/forward diagonally (makes a V)
3. Step RIGHT foot back to center
4. Step LEFT foot back to center

**Add Arms:**
- Arms punch UP on the out steps
- Arms DOWN on the back steps

**Make it 80s:**
- Add shoulder shimmies
- Snap fingers on the way back
- Make it SASSY!

*"Out-out-back-back!"*

---

### Move 3: Jumping Jacks with Variations (3 minutes)

**Classic Jumping Jacks** (32 counts)
- The OG aerobics move!
- Arms strong, feet wide

**Seal Jacks** (16 counts)
- Arms clap in FRONT instead of overhead
- Like a seal!

**Scissors** (16 counts)
- Jump to split stance (one foot forward, one back)
- Switch feet each jump
- Arms opposite to legs

**Cross Jacks** (16 counts)
- Cross feet in front of each other
- Cross arms across chest

*"Jump it out!"*

---

### Move 4: Step Touch with Style (3 minutes)

**Basic Step Touch:**
1. Step RIGHT, touch LEFT foot beside
2. Step LEFT, touch RIGHT foot beside
3. Continue side to side

**Add 80s Flair:**
- Snap on each touch
- Shoulder rolls while stepping
- Arms reach to each side
- Turn it into a walk forward, then back!

**Double Time:**
- Same move, twice as fast!
- Get that heart pumping!

*"Step-touch! Step-touch!"*

---

### Move 5: Knee Lifts & Kicks (3 minutes)

**Alternating Knee Lifts** (32 counts)
- Lift right knee, lower
- Lift left knee, lower
- Arms pump opposite

**Add the Twist:**
- Twist body toward the lifting knee
- Bring opposite elbow to knee
- Crunch that core!

**Front Kicks** (16 counts each leg)
- Kick right leg forward, not too high
- Arms punch forward with kick
- Switch to left leg

**Combo:**
- 4 knee lifts, 4 kicks
- Repeat!

*"Lift-lift-kick-kick!"*

## 80s Aerobics Combo Sequence (5 minutes)

**Put it all together!**

**Section A** (32 counts)
- 8 counts: Grapevine RIGHT
- 8 counts: Grapevine LEFT
- 8 counts: V-step
- 8 counts: V-step

**Section B** (32 counts)
- 8 counts: Jumping jacks
- 8 counts: Cross jacks
- 8 counts: Knee lifts
- 8 counts: Kicks

**Section C** (32 counts)
- 8 counts: Step-touch RIGHT
- 8 counts: Step-touch LEFT
- 8 counts: Step-touch double time!
- 8 counts: March in place

**REPEAT the whole combo 3x!**

*Add more enthusiasm each time!*

## Cool-Down: Soft Rock Stretch (5 minutes)

*Slow it down with a power ballad*

**Slow March** (30 seconds)
- Bring that heart rate down
- Deep breaths

**Overhead Stretch** (20 seconds)
- Reach high, lean right
- Hold
- Reach high, lean left
- Hold

**Quad Stretch** (30 seconds each)
- Hold foot behind you
- Balance like an aerobics star

**Hamstring Stretch** (30 seconds each)
- Foot forward, flex toe
- Lean into it

**Hip Flexor Stretch** (30 seconds each)
- Lunge position
- Gentle stretch

**Shoulder Stretch** (20 seconds each)
- Arm across body
- Opposite hand pulls

**Final Breathing** (30 seconds)
- Hands at heart
- Deep breath in...
- Exhale: "Great workout, everyone!"

## Modifications

### Lower Impact
- Step jacks instead of jumping jacks
- March instead of jog
- Smaller range of motion
- Take breaks as needed

### Higher Impact
- Higher knees, higher kicks
- Add jumps to everything
- Faster tempo
- Double the combos!
