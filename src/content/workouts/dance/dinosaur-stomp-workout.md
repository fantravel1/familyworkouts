## Time Machine Warm-Up (3 minutes)

### Activate the Time Machine!

**Starting Position**
- Stand in the middle of your space
- Close your eyes
- Take 3 deep breaths

**Time Travel!**
- Start walking in a circle
- "We're going back in time!"
- Walk faster and faster
- Jump up: "We're in the DINOSAUR AGE!"
- Look around: "What do you see?"

**Prehistoric Stretches**
- Reach up high: "Touch the tall trees!"
- Bend down low: "Look for fossils!"
- Twist around: "Watch for dinosaurs!"
- Shake out your body: "Get ready to MOVE!"

## Meet the Dinosaurs (14 minutes)

### ü¶ï BRACHIOSAURUS (Gentle Giant)
*"I'm as tall as a 4-story building!"*

**The Movement:**
1. Stand tall, reach arms WAAAY up
2. Tippy toes‚Äîbe as tall as possible!
3. Stretch neck: Look up at the treetops
4. Walk slowly with long steps
5. Reach up to "eat leaves" from tall trees
6. 1 minute of tall walking and reaching

**Dino Fact**: Brachiosaurus was 85 feet long and ate 400 pounds of plants daily!

---

### ü¶ñ T-REX (Fierce Hunter)
*"I have tiny arms but POWERFUL legs!"*

**The Movement:**
1. Tuck arms in close (tiny T-Rex arms!)
2. Fingers become "claws"
3. STOMP with powerful legs
4. Take BIG heavy steps
5. Practice your ROAR!
6. Chase something (sibling, parent, toy)
7. 1.5 minutes of stomping and roaring

**Dino Fact**: T-Rex had the strongest bite of any land animal ever‚Äî12,800 pounds of force!

---

### ü¶é VELOCIRAPTOR (Speedy Hunter)
*"I'm small but SUPER fast!"*

**The Movement:**
1. Crouch down low
2. Arms tucked like little wings
3. Run FAST on tip-toes
4. Quick, darting movements
5. Sudden stops and direction changes
6. Make hissing/clicking sounds
7. 1.5 minutes of quick running

**Dino Fact**: Velociraptors were only about 2 feet tall‚Äîthe size of a turkey!

---

### ü¶ï STEGOSAURUS (Armored Plant-Eater)
*"I have plates on my back and spikes on my tail!"*

**The Movement:**
1. Walk on all fours (hands and feet)
2. Move slowly and heavily
3. Swing your "spiked tail" (swing hips!)
4. "Plates" stand up (arch your back)
5. Pretend to munch on plants
6. 1 minute of crawling and munching

**Dino Fact**: Stegosaurus's brain was only the size of a walnut!

---

### ü¶Ö PTERODACTYL (Flying Reptile)
*"I soar through the sky!"*

**The Movement:**
1. Spread arms wide like wings
2. Flap arms up and down
3. Run and "fly" around the room
4. Swoop down low, rise up high
5. Land and fold your wings
6. Make "caw caw" sounds
7. 1.5 minutes of flying

**Dino Fact**: Pterodactyls weren't actually dinosaurs‚Äîthey were flying reptiles!

---

### üêä ANKYLOSAURUS (Tank Dinosaur)
*"Try to get me‚ÄîI'm covered in armor!"*

**The Movement:**
1. Get on hands and knees
2. Make yourself into a ball (turtle shell)
3. Roll slightly side to side
4. Swing your "club tail" (swing your leg)
5. Pop up, waddle, curl up again
6. 1 minute of waddling and protecting

**Dino Fact**: Ankylosaurus had a tail like a club that could break a T-Rex's leg!

---

### ü¶ï TRICERATOPS (Three-Horned Face)
*"I have three horns to protect myself!"*

**The Movement:**
1. Point fingers on forehead like horns
2. Lower head like ready to charge
3. STOMP feet and snort
4. Charge forward!
5. Pretend to munch on plants
6. 1 minute of charging and eating

**Dino Fact**: Triceratops' frill (the fan around its head) could be 3 feet wide!

---

### ü•ö BABY DINOSAUR (Hatching!)
*"Crack! Crack! I'm hatching!"*

**The Movement:**
1. Curl up very small on the ground
2. You're inside an egg!
3. Start to wiggle
4. Push against your shell
5. CRACK! Break through!
6. Stretch out: "I'm alive!"
7. Take your first wobbly steps
8. 1 minute of hatching and exploring

## Dinosaur Games (3 minutes)

### Dino Says
Like Simon Says:
- "Dino says... BE A T-REX!"
- "Dino says... FREEZE LIKE A FOSSIL!"
- Trick: "Be a Pterodactyl!" (No "Dino says!")

### Freeze Dance: Extinction Edition
- Dance like dinosaurs when music plays
- When music stops: FREEZE (you're a fossil!)
- Silliest frozen dinosaur wins!

### Dinosaur Parade
- Line up in a parade
- Each person picks a dinosaur
- March around showing your dinosaur
- ROAR as you pass each other!

## Cool-Down: Dinosaur Extinction (3 minutes)

*"The dinosaurs are getting sleepy..."*

### Slow Motion Dinosaur
- Choose your favorite dinosaur
- Do the movements in SLOW motion
- Slower... slower...
- 1 minute

### Fossil Rest
- Lie down on your back
- Spread out like a fossil
- Take deep breaths
- You're preserved in rock
- Stay very still
- 1 minute

### Time Machine Home
- Slowly stand up
- Walk in a circle
- "Traveling back to the present..."
- Slow down
- Stop
- "We're HOME!"
- Shake off the dinosaur dust

## Parent Tips

### For Toddlers (2-3)
- Simple movements only
- Lots of roaring
- Heavy on imagination
- Short sessions (5-10 min)
- Don't worry about specific dinosaurs

### For Preschool (3-5)
- Love learning dinosaur names
- Will request favorites repeatedly
- Add counting: "Stomp 10 times!"
- Make it a story adventure

### For Kids (6-8)
- Appreciate the real facts
- Can do longer sequences
- Will correct you on dinosaur details!
- Add obstacle courses
