# Golf Fitness for Families

Whether you're hitting the course, the driving range, or the backyard, this workout improves the three keys to golf: rotation, flexibility, and balance.

## The Golf Body

Golf requires:
- **Rotational power** for the swing
- **Core stability** for consistency
- **Hip mobility** for proper turn
- **Shoulder flexibility** for full backswing
- **Balance** for solid contact
- **Leg strength** for power ground up

## Warm-Up: First Tee Prep (4 minutes)

### Getting Loose

**Arm Swings** (30 seconds)
- Big swings across body
- Loosen shoulders
- Like a practice swing!

**Torso Rotations** (30 seconds)
- Feet planted
- Twist left and right
- Arms follow naturally

**Hip Circles** (30 seconds)
- Hands on hips
- Big circles both directions
- Loosen those hips!

**Club Stretch** (pretend club or broomstick) (1 minute)
- Hold overhead
- Lean left, lean right
- Rotation with arms up

**Practice Swings** (1 minute)
- Slow-motion golf swings
- Focus on smooth motion
- No ball, just movement
- Feel the muscles engage

**Walking in Place** (30 seconds)
- Get blood flowing
- Light movement
- Ready to work!

## Rotation Station (6 minutes)

### Core Golf Moves

**Seated Rotations** (1 minute)
- Sit tall, feet flat
- Hands on shoulders
- Rotate left fully
- Rotate right fully
- Feel the twist
- 20 total rotations

**Standing Golf Turns** (1.5 minutes)
- Address position (feet shoulder-width)
- Rotate into "backswing"
- Hold 3 seconds
- Return to address
- Rotate to "follow-through"
- Hold 3 seconds
- 10 complete swings

**Medicine Ball Twists** (or pretend) (1 minute)
- Feet shoulder-width
- Arms extended
- Twist right, twist left
- Power from core
- 20 twists

**Core Cable Rotation** (1 minute)
*No cable needed!*
- Stand sideways
- Pull across body (like starting lawn mower)
- Rotation against resistance
- 10 each direction

**Dead Bug Golf** (1.5 minutes)
- Lie on back
- Arms and legs up
- Lower opposite arm/leg
- Core stability work
- 8 each side

## Flexibility Focus (5 minutes)

### Golf-Specific Stretches

**90/90 Hip Stretch** (1 minute each side)
- One leg in front at 90 degrees
- Other leg behind at 90 degrees
- Sit tall
- Feel hip stretch
- Huge for golf turn!

**Thread the Needle** (1 minute)
- On hands and knees
- Thread right arm under left
- Rotate and stretch
- Switch sides
- Thoracic mobility!

**Lat Stretch** (30 seconds each side)
- Reach one arm up and over
- Lean away from arm
- Feel lat stretch
- Important for follow-through!

**Hip Flexor Lunge** (30 seconds each side)
- Lunge position
- Push hips forward
- Arms up if comfortable
- Opens up hip turn!

**Shoulder Stretch** (30 seconds each side)
- Arm across body
- Pull at elbow
- Feel shoulder stretch
- Full backswing requires this!

## Balance Training (4 minutes)

### Steady Over the Ball

**Single Leg Balance** (1 minute each leg)
- Stand on one foot
- Other foot slightly raised
- Hold steady
- Add arm movements for challenge

**Swing Balance** (1 minute)
- Do practice swing
- Freeze in finish position
- Hold 10 seconds
- Repeat 5 times

**Stork Stand** (30 seconds each)
- Stand on one leg
- Other knee raised high
- Flamingo position
- Golf tee balance!

**Eyes Closed Balance** (1 minute)
- Both feet on floor
- Close eyes
- Maintain balance
- Really challenges stability!

## Strength for Golf (4 minutes)

### Power Development

**Goblet Squats** (1 minute)
- Feet shoulder-width
- Hands at chest
- Squat with flat back
- 12-15 reps
- Leg power for swing!

**Glute Bridges** (1 minute)
- Lie on back
- Feet flat on floor
- Lift hips
- Squeeze glutes
- 15 reps
- Hip power!

**Push-Ups** (1 minute)
- Any variation
- Full or modified
- 10-15 reps
- Upper body strength!

**Planks** (1 minute)
- Hold plank position
- Core engaged
- 30-45 second holds
- Stability for consistency!

## Cool-Down: 19th Hole (3 minutes)

### Recovery

**Standing Forward Fold** (30 seconds)
- Bend forward
- Let arms hang
- Relax back muscles

**Cross-Body Stretch** (30 seconds each)
- Pull arm across
- Shoulder stretch
- Both sides

**Quad Stretch** (30 seconds each)
- Pull foot to glutes
- Balance or hold something
- Both legs

**Side Stretch** (30 seconds each)
- Reach up and over
- Both sides
- Final stretch

**Deep Breathing** (30 seconds)
- Stand tall
- Deep breaths
- Ready for the first tee!

## Golf-Specific Tips

### For Juniors
- Focus on fun over form
- Shorter exercises
- Balance games
- Rotation through play

### For Adults
- Full workout as written
- Add weights when ready
- Focus on weak areas
- Consistency matters

### For Seniors
- Chair-supported balance
- Gentle rotations
- Flexibility priority
- Keep it safe

## Pre-Round Routine

### 5 Minutes Before Tee Time

1. Arm swings (30 sec)
2. Torso rotations (30 sec)
3. Hip circles (30 sec)
4. Practice swings (1 min)
5. Balance holds (30 sec)
6. Deep breaths (30 sec)

*Do this before every round!*

## Benefits for Your Game

| Exercise | Golf Benefit |
|----------|--------------|
| Rotations | More clubhead speed |
| Hip stretches | Full shoulder turn |
| Balance work | Solid contact |
| Core work | Consistency |
| Leg strength | Power from ground |
| Flexibility | Prevent injury |
