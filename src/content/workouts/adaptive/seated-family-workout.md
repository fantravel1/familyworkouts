## Everyone Moves, Everyone Belongs

This workout ensures every family member can participate together, regardless of mobility level. Whether you're a grandparent, recovering from injury, using a wheelchair, or simply prefer seated exerciseâ€”you're doing the SAME workout as everyone else.

## Why Seated Workouts Matter

- **Truly inclusive**: No one sits out
- **Multi-generational**: Perfect for family reunions
- **Effective**: Real exercise, real benefits
- **Safe**: Lower fall risk
- **Accessible**: Minimal equipment needed

## What You Need

- **Sturdy chair** (no wheels!)
- **Comfortable clothes**
- **Water nearby**
- **Optional**: Light weights, water bottles, or resistance bands

## Setting Up

- Chair on non-slip surface
- Feet flat on floor (or footrests)
- Sit toward front of chair
- Back supported but posture tall
- Everyone in a circle facing each other

## The Workout

### Warm-Up: Wake Up the Body (3 minutes)

**Seated March**
- Lift knees alternately
- Pump arms
- 30 seconds
- "Let's get moving!"

**Shoulder Rolls**
- Big circles backward
- Big circles forward
- 10 each direction
- "Release that tension!"

**Neck Stretches**
- Ear to right shoulder, hold 5 seconds
- Ear to left shoulder, hold 5 seconds
- Look right, hold 5 seconds
- Look left, hold 5 seconds
- Chin to chest, hold 5 seconds
- "Gentle and easy!"

**Arm Reaches**
- Reach high overhead, stretch
- Reach forward, stretch
- Reach to right side, stretch
- Reach to left side, stretch
- 3 rounds

---

### Cardio Section (5 minutes)

**Seated Jumping Jacks**
- Start: arms at sides, legs together
- Open: arms overhead, legs apart (tap out)
- Return to start
- 30 seconds

**Punches**
- Punch right arm forward
- Punch left arm forward
- Alternate with power!
- 30 seconds

**Knee Lifts with Arm Press**
- Lift right knee, press arms up
- Lower knee, lower arms
- Lift left knee, press arms up
- Continue alternating
- 45 seconds

**Seated Running**
- Quick feet marching
- Pump arms fast
- Stay seated but move quickly!
- 30 seconds

**Rest and breathe** - 15 seconds

**Boxing Combo**
- Right punch, left punch
- Right uppercut, left uppercut
- Repeat with energy!
- 45 seconds

**Cross-Body Knee Touch**
- Right elbow to left knee
- Left elbow to right knee
- Twist through core
- 30 seconds

---

### Strength Section (7 minutes)

**Chair Push-Ups**
- Hands on armrests (or seat edge)
- Push down to lift body slightly
- Lower with control
- 10 repetitions
- Rest 15 seconds
- 2 sets

**Seated Leg Extensions**
- Extend right leg straight
- Hold 3 seconds, feel quad working
- Lower slowly
- 10 each leg
- Add ankle weights for challenge

**Seated Knee Squeezes**
- Place ball or pillow between knees
- Squeeze and hold 5 seconds
- Release
- 10 repetitions
- Works inner thighs

**Arm Curls**
- Use weights, water bottles, or resistance
- Curl both arms up
- Lower slowly
- 12 repetitions
- 2 sets

**Overhead Press**
- Weights at shoulders
- Press straight up
- Lower to shoulders
- 12 repetitions
- 2 sets

**Seated Row**
- Arms forward, fists together
- Pull elbows back, squeeze shoulder blades
- Return forward
- 15 repetitions
- Imaginary rowing!

**Toe Raises**
- Heels on floor
- Lift toes as high as possible
- Lower
- 15 repetitions
- Works shins

**Heel Raises**
- Toes on floor
- Lift heels as high as possible
- Lower
- 15 repetitions
- Works calves

---

### Core Section (3 minutes)

**Seated Twist**
- Arms crossed at chest
- Twist right, return center
- Twist left, return center
- Keep hips forward, twist from waist
- 20 total (10 each side)

**Seated Lean Back**
- Hands on thighs for support
- Lean back slightly, engage core
- Hold 5 seconds
- Return to upright
- 8 repetitions
- Don't hold breath!

**Knee Lift Hold**
- Lift both knees slightly off seat
- Hold 5 seconds
- Lower
- 6 repetitions
- Builds core stability

**Side Bends**
- Right arm reaches toward floor
- Feel left side stretch
- Return to center
- Repeat left side
- 10 each side

---

### Cool-Down: Gentle Stretches (2 minutes)

**Ankle Circles**
- Lift right foot, circle ankle
- 5 circles each direction
- Repeat left foot
- Releases ankle tension

**Figure 4 Stretch**
- Right ankle on left knee
- Sit tall, gentle lean forward
- Feel hip stretch
- Hold 20 seconds each side

**Chest Opener**
- Clasp hands behind back
- Squeeze shoulder blades together
- Lift chest
- Hold 15 seconds

**Shoulder Stretch**
- Right arm across chest
- Left hand holds right elbow
- Hold 15 seconds each side

**Deep Breaths**
- Inhale slowly, arms rise
- Exhale slowly, arms lower
- 5 rounds
- "We did it together!"

## Modifications & Progressions

### Making It Easier
- Smaller range of motion
- Slower pace
- Skip weights
- More rest between exercises
- Focus on upper OR lower body

### Making It Harder
- Larger range of motion
- Faster pace (with control)
- Add weights
- More repetitions
- Combine upper and lower body moves

### Wheelchair Adaptations
- All upper body exercises work great
- Secure wheelchair brakes
- Push against armrests for "push-ups"
- Core work focuses on twists and leans
- Arm cardio provides full workout

---

## Multi-Generational Tips

### When Grandparents Join
- They set the pace
- Celebrate their participation
- Ask about exercises from their generation
- Share stories during rest breaks

### When Kids Join
- Give them special "jobs" (count reps, pick music)
- Let them add silly variations
- Praise their patience with slower pace
- Explain why we adapt for everyone

### Mixed Ability Groups
- Everyone does same exercises
- Each person at their own level
- Focus on togetherness, not performance
- "We're all doing this TOGETHER"

## Important Safety Notes

1. **Medical clearance**: Check with doctor if new to exercise
2. **Stable chair**: No wheels, on non-slip surface
3. **Posture**: Sit tall, don't slouch
4. **Breathing**: Never hold your breath
5. **Listen to your body**: Pain means stop
6. **Hydrate**: Water before, during, after
