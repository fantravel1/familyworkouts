# Preschool Movement Magic

Welcome to the most magical workout for our littlest movers! Simple, fun, and full of imagination‚Äîbecause at this age, play IS exercise.

## The Preschool Philosophy

At ages 3-5, children need:
- **Unstructured play**: Not every moment needs instruction
- **Simple movements**: Building blocks for later skills
- **Imagination**: Make-believe makes exercise fun
- **Short bursts**: Attention spans are short
- **Lots of praise**: Every attempt is celebrated!

## Magical Warm-Up (3 minutes)

### Wake Up the Magic!

**Fairy Dust Sprinkles** (30 seconds)
- Pretend to sprinkle fairy dust
- On your head!
- On your tummy!
- On your toes!
- Shake it off!

**Balloon Body** (30 seconds)
- Take a BIIIG breath
- Get BIG like a balloon!
- Hold it...
- Blow out and get small!
- Repeat!

**Wiggly Worms** (30 seconds)
- Wiggle your fingers
- Wiggle your arms
- Wiggle your whole body!
- Silly wiggles everywhere!

**Stompy Dinosaurs** (30 seconds)
- STOMP like a big dinosaur!
- ROAR!
- Stomp around the room!
- ROOOOAR!

**Twinkle Stars** (30 seconds)
- Hands open and close (twinkle!)
- Reach up to the sky
- Twinkle, twinkle!
- You're a star!

**Silly Shakes** (30 seconds)
- Shake your hands
- Shake your feet
- Shake your bottom!
- Shake everything!

## Movement Story Time (5 minutes)

### The Adventure in the Forest

*Read this as you act it out together!*

---

"Once upon a time, you were a **tiny seed** in the ground..."
- *Curl up in a little ball*

"The sun came out and you started to **GROW**..."
- *Slowly stand up, reaching arms high*

"You grew into a TALL TREE with big branches..."
- *Arms wide, standing tall, swaying gently*

"Then a bird flew by!"
- *Flap your arms like wings*

"The bird landed on your branch..."
- *Stand on one foot, arms out for balance*

"WHOOSH! The wind blew strong!"
- *Sway side to side*

"The wind blew so hard, all your leaves fell off!"
- *Wiggle fingers down to the ground*

"You had to pick them all up!"
- *Bend down, pretend to pick up leaves*

"Then you saw a BUNNY hop by!"
- *Hop like a bunny*

"And a BEAR walking through the forest!"
- *Walk like a big heavy bear*

"The bear was so tired, he laid down to sleep..."
- *Lie down, pretend to snore*

"WAKE UP! The sun is out!"
- *Jump up and stretch!*

"THE END! Give yourself a big hug!"
- *Hug yourself!*

---

## Animal Friends (3 minutes)

### Move Like the Animals!

**Froggy Jumps** (30 seconds)
- Squat down like a frog
- JUMP!
- Ribbit ribbit!
- Jump as high as you can!

**Kitty Cat Stretches** (30 seconds)
- On hands and knees
- Arch your back like a scared cat
- Drop your tummy like a happy cat
- Meow!

**Puppy Dog Wags** (30 seconds)
- On hands and knees
- Wag your bottom like a tail!
- Pant like a happy puppy!
- Woof woof!

**Elephant Stomps** (30 seconds)
- Stomp with HEAVY feet
- Swing your trunk (arm) back and forth
- Make elephant sounds!

**Butterfly Wings** (30 seconds)
- Arms out like wings
- Flutter up and down
- Fly around the room!
- Land on a flower (sit down gently)

**Snake Slithers** (30 seconds)
- Lie on your tummy
- Wiggle like a snake
- Ssssssss!
- No arms, just wiggle!

## Color Dance (2 minutes)

### When You See the Color, Move!

**Parent/Adult leads by calling out colors:**

"When I say RED, you... STOMP YOUR FEET!"
- Practice: RED! (stomp stomp stomp)

"When I say BLUE, you... SPIN AROUND!"
- Practice: BLUE! (spin spin)

"When I say YELLOW, you... JUMP UP HIGH!"
- Practice: YELLOW! (jump jump)

"When I say GREEN, you... FREEZE LIKE A STATUE!"
- Practice: GREEN! (freeze!)

**Play the Game:**
- Call colors in random order
- Go faster and faster!
- Silly mistakes are fine!
- Laugh and have fun!

## Counting Moves (2 minutes)

### Exercise with Numbers!

**Let's count to 5 with our bodies!**

**1 - ONE jump!** (one big jump)

**2 - TWO claps!** (clap clap)

**3 - THREE spins!** (spin, spin, spin)

**4 - FOUR stomps!** (stomp stomp stomp stomp)

**5 - FIVE wiggles!** (wiggle wiggle wiggle wiggle wiggle)

**Now let's do it all together!**
- 1 jump!
- 2 claps!
- 3 spins!
- 4 stomps!
- 5 wiggles!

**YAY! You did it!**

## Cool-Down Magic (2 minutes)

### Time to Rest

**Cloud Floating** (30 seconds)
- Lie on your back
- Pretend you're floating on a fluffy cloud
- Soft and relaxed
- Look at the sky

**Deep Breathing Bubbles** (30 seconds)
- Take a big breath in
- Blow out like you're blowing bubbles
- Biiiiig bubbles!
- Slow and easy

**Goodnight Stars** (30 seconds)
- Curl up cozy
- The stars are coming out
- Close your eyes
- Twinkle twinkle (quiet voice)

**Big Wake Up** (30 seconds)
- Stretch like you're waking up
- Stretch your arms
- Stretch your legs
- BIG yawn!
- Good morning!

## Making It Work

### Best Times to Exercise
- After a snack (energy!)
- Before nap (tire them out!)
- When they're being wild (channel energy!)
- NOT when hungry or tired

### Best Spaces
- Living room cleared
- Backyard
- Basement
- Anywhere safe to move!

### Best Music
- Kids' songs they know
- Upbeat and fun
- Not too loud
- Something they can sing along to

## Simple Equipment Ideas

### Household Items
- **Pillows**: Jump over, crash into
- **Blankets**: Capes, parachutes, tunnels
- **Stuffed animals**: Dance partners, obstacles
- **Balloons**: Keep-up games
- **Boxes**: Tunnels, stepping stones

### No Equipment Needed!
- Imagination is the best toy
- Your body does everything
- Make-believe is exercise!

## Celebrating Movement

### Simple Rewards
- High fives
- Silly dances
- Stickers
- Verbal praise ("Wow, you jumped so high!")
- Hugs

### No Food Rewards
- Don't tie exercise to treats
- Movement is its own reward
- Keep associations positive

## Preschool Exercise Goals

### What They're Learning
- Basic coordination
- Body awareness
- Following simple instructions
- Moving with others
- Confidence in their bodies

### What You're Building
- Love of movement
- Positive exercise associations
- Family fitness time
- Healthy habits early
