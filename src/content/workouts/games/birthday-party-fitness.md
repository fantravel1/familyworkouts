# Birthday Party Fitness Bash

Who says party time can't be active time? This collection of games and activities turns any birthday celebration into a movement-filled extravaganza!

## Why Active Parties?

- **Burns birthday cake calories** (not really the goal, but bonus!)
- **Keeps kids entertained**: Active kids = happy kids
- **Less chaos**: Structured activities = manageable party
- **Inclusive fun**: Everyone can participate
- **Memorable**: Kids will talk about this party forever!

## Party Setup

### Space Needed
- Living room cleared
- Backyard
- Party venue with open space
- Basement playroom

### Materials
- Balloons (lots!)
- Music and speakers
- Party decorations (use as markers!)
- Small prizes (optional)
- Timer or phone

## Opening Ceremony (5 minutes)

### The Grand Arrival

**Birthday Star Entrance** (2 minutes)
1. Birthday child stands in center
2. Everyone circles around
3. March in a circle singing "Happy Birthday"
4. March speeds up!
5. Everyone cheers!

**Guest Warm-Up Dance** (3 minutes)
1. Play upbeat music
2. Everyone dances in
3. Free movement
4. Getting energized for the party!

## Game Station 1: Balloon Bonanza (10 minutes)

### Balloon-Based Active Games

**Balloon Keep-Up** (3 minutes)
*Classic party game with fitness twist!*

1. Each team gets a balloon
2. Keep it in the air!
3. Must hit it at least every 3 seconds
4. Do 5 jumping jacks every time it falls!
5. Most keeps in 3 minutes wins

**Balloon Waddle Race** (3 minutes)
*Hilarious and physical!*

1. Balloon between the knees
2. Waddle to the finish line
3. No hands allowed!
4. If balloon drops, start over
5. Relay race style!

**Pop and Drop** (2 minutes)
*High energy finale!*

1. Balloons everywhere on the floor
2. Everyone sits on balloons to pop them
3. Pop as many as you can!
4. Sitting, jumping, however you can!
5. *Note: Some kids may find popping scary - have alternative task*

**Balloon Volleyball** (2 minutes)
1. Two teams
2. String or pillows as "net"
3. Balloon must stay in air
4. Team that lets it touch ground does 5 squats
5. First to 5 points wins!

## Game Station 2: Dance Party Games (10 minutes)

### Get Those Bodies Moving!

**Freeze Dance** (3 minutes)
*The ultimate party game!*

1. Music plays, everyone dances
2. Music stops, FREEZE!
3. Anyone moving sits out
4. Last person standing wins!
5. Silly poses encouraged!

**Dance Move Copycat** (3 minutes)
*Follow the leader, dance edition!*

1. Birthday child starts as leader
2. Does a dance move
3. Everyone copies
4. Leader tags next person
5. New leader, new move!

**Musical Statues with Themes** (2 minutes)
1. Music plays, dance!
2. When music stops, freeze as...
3. "Superhero pose!"
4. "Silly face!"
5. "Favorite animal!"
6. Best pose gets a point!

**Dance Circle** (2 minutes)
1. Everyone in a circle
2. One person goes in center
3. Shows off moves for 20 seconds
4. Everyone cheers!
5. Next person jumps in!

## Game Station 3: Racing Relays (8 minutes)

### Team Competition Time!

**Obstacle Course Relay** (3 minutes)
Set up simple course:
1. Jump over pillows
2. Crawl under string/blanket
3. Spin around 3 times
4. Run back, tag next person
5. First team done wins!

**Crab Walk Race** (2 minutes)
1. Teams line up
2. Crab walk to marker and back
3. Tag next teammate
4. Hilarious to watch!

**Animal Relay** (3 minutes)
Different movement each lap:
- Lap 1: Bear crawl
- Lap 2: Bunny hop
- Lap 3: Frog jump
- Lap 4: Run!
First team through all animals wins!

## Game Station 4: Classic Party Games - Active Edition (8 minutes)

### Traditional Games with a Twist

**Musical Chairs - Exercise Edition** (3 minutes)
1. Traditional musical chairs BUT
2. Instead of sitting when eliminated...
3. Do 10 jumping jacks to rejoin!
4. Everyone plays the whole time!

**Hot Potato Workout** (3 minutes)
1. Pass object while music plays
2. When music stops, person holding...
3. Leads the group in 5 of an exercise
4. They pick: jumping jacks, squats, etc.
5. Keep passing!

**Red Light, Green Light - Extreme** (2 minutes)
1. Normal red light, green light
2. Green light = RUN
3. Yellow light = slow motion
4. Red light = FREEZE and do a pose!
5. First to finish line wins

## Game Station 5: Team Challenges (5 minutes)

### Working Together

**Human Knot** (2 minutes)
1. Circle up, grab hands across
2. Without letting go...
3. Untangle into a circle!
4. Teamwork and stretching!

**Group Jump Challenge** (1.5 minutes)
1. Everyone holds hands in a line
2. Try to jump together
3. Count how many in a row
4. Beat your record!

**Freeze Frame Challenge** (1.5 minutes)
1. Team creates a "scene" together
2. Like a photograph
3. Other team guesses what it is
4. Movies, activities, etc.
5. Lots of posing and moving!

## Birthday Cake Dance Break (5 minutes)

### The Cake Ritual - Active Version!

**Candle Blow Training** (1 minute)
1. Deep breath in
2. Blow out imaginary candles
3. Practice for the real thing!
4. Silly breathing exercises

**Wish Jump** (1 minute)
1. Everyone thinks of a wish
2. JUMP as high as you can!
3. Wishes fly up!
4. Happy energy!

**Cake Walk** (3 minutes)
1. Numbers on floor in a circle
2. Walk around while music plays
3. When music stops, stand on number
4. Random number called = winner!
5. Classic carnival game!

## Cool-Down: Party Wind-Down (4 minutes)

### Calming Before Cake

**Gratitude Circle** (1 minute)
1. Sit in a circle
2. Each person says one thing they liked
3. Birthday child goes last
4. Building community!

**Gentle Stretches** (2 minutes)
1. Seated stretches
2. Calm music
3. Preparing for food!
4. Energy coming down

**Group Photo Pose Practice** (1 minute)
1. Practice silly poses
2. Get ready for photos!
3. Best pose contest
4. Captures the memories!

## Age-Specific Modifications

### Toddler Party (2-3 years)
- Simpler games
- More free play
- Parachute activities
- Bubble chasing
- Short attention span activities

### Preschool Party (4-5 years)
- All games work!
- More help needed with rules
- Extra praise and encouragement
- Everyone wins approach

### Elementary Party (6-10 years)
- Full games as written
- Add competition elements
- Teams work great
- More complex rules okay

### Tween Party (11-13 years)
- More challenging games
- Dance competitions
- Obstacle course races
- Scavenger hunts
- Less "kiddie" themes

## Party Themes & Matching Games

### Superhero Party
- "Save the City" relay race
- Superhero pose freeze dance
- Villain chase tag
- Power training exercises

### Princess/Prince Party
- Royal ball dancing
- Tower climb (stairs if available)
- Dragon chase game
- Crown balancing race

### Sports Party
- Mini Olympics
- Sport station rotations
- Medal ceremony
- Team challenges

### Space Party
- Moon walk races
- Asteroid dodge
- Rocket launches (jumping)
- Zero gravity movements

### Animal Party
- Animal movement relay
- Zoo escape game
- Animal freeze dance
- Safari hunt

## Indoor vs. Outdoor Options

### Indoor Alternatives
- Smaller movement games
- More dance activities
- Balloon games work great
- Crawling and floor games

### Outdoor Bonuses
- Larger races
- More running games
- Water elements (summer)
- Bigger obstacle courses

## Prizes & Rewards

### Active Participation Prizes
- Everyone gets something!
- "Best dancer" ribbon
- "Fastest runner" certificate
- "Most enthusiastic" award
- No one left out!

### No-Prize Alternative
- Celebrate DOING over WINNING
- Cheer for everyone
- Photo with birthday child
- High fives all around

## Parent Tips

### Before the Party
- Clear space well
- Have backup indoor games
- Prep music playlist
- Brief helpers on games

### During the Party
- Rotate games quickly
- Watch energy levels
- Have water breaks
- Be flexible!

### After Active Play
- Transition to calm
- Food/cake time
- Presents if doing
- Goodbye activities

## Printable Game Cards

### Quick Reference for Party Helpers

**Balloon Keep-Up**
- Goal: Keep balloon up
- Time: 3 min
- Players: Teams of 4
- If falls: 5 jumping jacks

**Freeze Dance**
- Goal: Don't move when music stops
- Time: Until one winner
- Players: Everyone
- Move = Sit out

**Musical Chairs Exercise**
- Goal: Grab a chair
- Time: Until one winner
- No chair = 10 jumping jacks then rejoin!
