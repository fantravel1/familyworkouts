# Simon Says Fitness Frenzy

"Simon says... get ready for the best workout ever!" This classic game becomes an epic exercise session that tricks kids into serious fitness.

## Why This Works

- **Listening skills** - Must pay attention to succeed
- **Full-body workout** - Different exercises target everything
- **Infinite variations** - Never gets boring
- **Zero equipment** - Play anywhere, anytime
- **All ages welcome** - Modify for anyone

## Basic Rules Refresher

1. One person is "Simon" (the leader)
2. If Simon says "Simon says jump," everyone jumps
3. If Simon just says "Jump," nobody should move!
4. Keep playingâ€”no eliminations in fitness version
5. Rotate who plays Simon

## Trick Strategies for Simon

### Speed Tricks
- Say commands faster and faster
- Pause unexpectedly between commands
- Whisper some commands

### Rhythm Tricks
- Establish a pattern, then break it
- "Simon says" three times, then skip it
- Use similar sounding words

### Confusion Tricks
- "Simon says don't jump!" (What do you do?)
- "Simon says stop... doing push-ups!" (Were you doing them?)
- Combine two movements: "Simon says pat your head and rub your tummy!"

## Scoring (Optional)

### Point System
- Complete exercise correctly: +1 point
- Caught moving without "Simon says": -1 point
- Perfect round (no mistakes): +5 bonus points

### Fitness Scoring (Non-Competitive)
- Track total exercises completed
- Try to beat family record
- Celebrate improvement, not winners

### Teens & Adults

## Pro Tips

1. **Energy matching**: Start calm, build up, calm down
2. **Voice variety**: Whisper, shout, use funny voices
3. **Movement demos**: Show while saying (especially for little ones)
4. **Rotate quickly**: Everyone wants to be Simon!
5. **Add music**: Background music increases energy
6. **Use props**: Simon holds a special hat or wand
7. **Celebrate catches**: "You caught me!" builds engagement
