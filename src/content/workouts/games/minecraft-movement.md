# Minecraft Movement Adventure

Welcome to the Overworld! Today we'll mine, craft, survive, and build our way to fitness. Every block counts!

## Survival Mode Activated

In this workout, you'll:
- Mine for resources (strength exercises)
- Craft tools (combination movements)
- Fight mobs (cardio bursts)
- Build structures (endurance holds)

## Choose Your Skin

Pick your player style:
- **Steve** - Classic, balanced workout
- **Alex** - Agile, quick movements
- **Zombie** - Slow, heavy movements
- **Skeleton** - Light, archer-style
- **Enderman** - Tall stretches, teleporting moves

## Spawn Point: Warm-Up (3 minutes)

### First Day in Minecraft

**Punch a Tree** (45 seconds)
- Punching motion with alternating arms
- You need wood to start!
- Keep punching until you get logs!

**Look Around** (30 seconds)
- Head turns left and right
- Scanning for mobs
- Staying alert!

**Jump for Apples** (45 seconds)
- Jump up reaching overhead
- Grabbing apples from trees
- Food for your health bar!

**Crouch/Sneak Mode** (30 seconds)
- Walk while in squat position
- Sneaking like in the game
- Don't fall off the edge!

**Sprint Jump** (30 seconds)
- Hop forward while jogging
- Minecraft sprint-jumping!
- Cover more ground!

## Mining Session (6 minutes)

### Going Underground

**Stone Mining Squats** (1 minute)
*Mining stone for basic tools*
- Squat down (pickaxe swing down)
- Stand up (lift the block)
- "Clink, clink, clink" sounds!
- 15-20 mining squats

**Iron Ore Lunges** (1 minute)
*Digging deeper for iron!*
- Forward lunge (dig forward)
- Push back (check inventory)
- Alternate legs
- 10 each leg

**Gold Mining Side Lunges** (1 minute)
*Side shafts for gold!*
- Side lunge left (mine left)
- Side lunge right (mine right)
- Arms make pickaxe motion
- 8 each side

**Diamond Mining Deep Squats** (1 minute)
*You found diamonds! Dig carefully!*
- Deep squat and hold
- Careful mining motion
- Don't break the diamonds!
- Hold 3 seconds at bottom, 10 reps

**Lava Jump Escape** (1 minute)
*You hit lava! Get out!*
- Quick jumps in place
- Escape the lava!
- High intensity!
- 20 panic jumps

**Resurface Sprint** (1 minute)
*Time to go back up!*
- Sprint in place (running up stairs)
- Place torches (arm reaches)
- Don't let monsters catch you!

## Crafting Time (4 minutes)

### Making Your Gear

**Crafting Table Arm Circles** (45 seconds)
*Arranging items on the crafting grid*
- Big arm circles
- Placing items in the grid
- Forward and backward

**Sword Crafting Chops** (45 seconds)
*Making your diamond sword!*
- Chopping motion overhead
- Like swinging a sword down
- Build that weapon!

**Armor Crafting Press** (45 seconds)
*Pressing armor into shape*
- Push-up motion (floor or wall)
- Pressing the chestplate
- 10 crafting presses

**Shield Crafting Holds** (45 seconds)
*Holding the shield in place*
- Plank hold position
- Steady like a shield
- Hold strong!

**Bow and Arrow Pulls** (45 seconds)
*Crafting your bow*
- Pull back motion (archer)
- Feel the stretch
- Both arms, alternate

## Mob Battle! (5 minutes)

### Night Has Fallen!

**Zombie Defense** (45 seconds)
*Zombies are slow but keep coming!*
- Slow, heavy punches
- Step back, punch forward
- "Uuuugh" zombie sounds optional!

**Skeleton Dodge and Strike** (45 seconds)
*Dodge arrows, attack back!*
- Side step (dodge!)
- Punch forward (attack!)
- Quick movements!

**Spider Jump Attacks** (45 seconds)
*Spiders are jumping at you!*
- Jump to stomp spiders
- Arms swatting
- They're everywhere!

**Creeper Escape!** (45 seconds)
*HSSSSSS - RUN!*
- Sprint in place!
- It's gonna blow!
- Maximum speed escape!
- "BOOM!" then slow down (you survived!)

**Enderman Freeze** (45 seconds)
*Don't look at it! FREEZE!*
- Complete stillness
- Statue hold
- Don't. Move.
- Hold as long as possible!

**Boss Battle: Wither** (1 minute)
*Ultimate mob fight!*
- All-out effort
- Punching, jumping, running
- Give it everything!
- Defeat the Wither!

## Building Mode (4 minutes)

### Constructing Your Base

**Foundation Plank** (1 minute)
*Building a strong foundation*
- Plank hold
- Flat and stable
- Strong base for your house!

**Wall Building Squats** (1 minute)
*Stacking blocks for walls*
- Squat (pick up block)
- Stand and reach up (place block)
- Wall goes higher and higher!
- 15 wall-building reps

**Roof Reaches** (1 minute)
*Stretching to build the roof*
- Reach high overhead
- Stretch side to side
- Placing roof blocks
- Don't fall!

**Door Frame Holds** (1 minute)
*Standing in your doorway*
- Stand tall in doorframe pose
- Feet grounded
- Arms reaching to sides
- Guardian of your home!

## Cool-Down: Peaceful Mode (3 minutes)

### Sun is Rising

**Slow Farm Walk** (1 minute)
- Walking slowly in place
- Tending your farm
- Peaceful vibes
- Heart rate lowering

**Fishing Stretch** (1 minute)
- Seated position
- Cast your line (arm reach forward)
- Reel in slowly
- Gentle movement

**Bed Rest** (1 minute)
- Lie down (like sleeping in Minecraft)
- Deep breaths
- Set your spawn point
- Relax and recover

## Difficulty Modes

### Peaceful Mode (Easy)
- No mob battles
- Longer rest between sections
- Focus on mining and building
- Perfect for beginners

### Easy Mode
- Shorter mob battles
- Standard rest periods
- Full workout, moderate intensity

### Normal Mode
- As written above
- Full experience
- Good challenge!

### Hard Mode
- Extended mob battles
- Shorter rest periods
- Double the mining
- For experienced players!

### Hardcore Mode
- No rest between sections
- Maximum reps
- Extended battles
- One "life" - any break = restart!

## Multiplayer Mode

### Co-op Building Challenge
- Work together to "build" (complete exercises)
- Count total family reps
- Build the ultimate base together!

### PvP Arena
- Compete during mob battles
- Who lasts longest?
- Who does most reps?
- Track XP points!

### Speedrun Challenge
- Time the full workout
- Try to beat your record
- Family speedrun competition!
