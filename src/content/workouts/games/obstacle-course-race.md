# DIY Obstacle Course Race

Channel your inner ninja warrior! Build epic obstacle courses using everyday items for the ultimate family fitness challenge.

## The Obstacle Course Philosophy

Obstacle courses combine:
- **Cardio**: Running between stations
- **Strength**: Climbing, lifting, carrying
- **Agility**: Quick direction changes
- **Balance**: Unstable surfaces
- **Problem-solving**: Figuring out obstacles

## Indoor Course Design

### The Classic 10-Station Course

**Station 1: Tunnel Crawl**
*Equipment: Chairs + blanket*
- Drape blanket over line of chairs
- Crawl through the tunnel
- Time: 10-15 seconds

**Station 2: Pillow Path**
*Equipment: 6-8 pillows*
- Scatter pillows as stepping stones
- Step only on pillows (floor is lava!)
- Time: 15-20 seconds

**Station 3: Chair Limbo**
*Equipment: Two chairs + pool noodle/broom*
- Balance noodle between chairs
- Limbo underneath
- Lower each round!
- Time: 5-10 seconds

**Station 4: Laundry Basket Toss**
*Equipment: Basket + 3 soft balls/socks*
- Stand 6 feet away
- Toss balls into basket
- Must make 2 of 3
- Time: 15-20 seconds

**Station 5: Couch Mountain**
*Equipment: Couch cushions stacked*
- Climb over the mountain
- Or: Army crawl across
- Time: 10-15 seconds

**Station 6: Zigzag Sprint**
*Equipment: 6 cones/objects in zigzag*
- Sprint weaving through markers
- Touch ground at each turn
- Time: 15-20 seconds

**Station 7: Balance Beam**
*Equipment: Tape line on floor*
- Walk the line heel-to-toe
- Arms out for balance
- Fall off = restart
- Time: 10-15 seconds

**Station 8: Bear Crawl Zone**
*Equipment: 10-foot clear space*
- Bear crawl across space
- Hands and feet only
- Time: 15-20 seconds

**Station 9: Jump & Reach**
*Equipment: Balloon or high object*
- Jump to touch ceiling/balloon
- 5 successful jumps
- Time: 15-20 seconds

**Station 10: Victory Sprint**
*Equipment: Clear path*
- Sprint to finish line
- Ring bell/slap wall
- DONE!
- Time: 5-10 seconds

**Total Course Time Goal: 3-5 minutes**

## Outdoor Course Design

### Backyard Ninja Course

**Station 1: Garden Gate Start**
- Sprint from starting line
- Touch the gate
- Run back through

**Station 2: Tire Steps** (or hula hoops)
- Jump into each tire/hoop
- One foot per tire
- Don't touch edges!

**Station 3: Water Bucket Carry**
- Pick up bucket (partially filled)
- Walk to next station
- Don't spill!

**Station 4: Low Crawl**
- Under rope or table
- Army crawl style
- Stay low!

**Station 5: Ball Throw**
- Throw ball into target
- Basketball hoop, bucket, or box
- Must make it!

**Station 6: Wall Climb** (or fence touch)
- Scale the wall safely
- Or: 5 wall touches
- Or: Climb play structure

**Station 7: Cargo Net** (or ladder climb)
- Climb rope ladder
- Or climb regular ladder
- Or climb jungle gym

**Station 8: Monkey Bars** (or arm hang)
- Cross monkey bars
- Or: Hang for 15 seconds
- Or: Tree hang

**Station 9: Log Balance**
- Walk along log/beam
- 2x4 board works
- Steady and slow

**Station 10: Final Sprint**
- All-out run to finish!
- Ring the bell!
- CHAMPION!

## Course Variations

### Speed Course (Quick Version)
- 5 stations only
- Focus on fast completion
- Best of 3 attempts
- Track times!

### Endurance Course (Long Version)
- 15+ stations
- Lower intensity per station
- Complete twice through
- Stamina challenge!

### Team Relay Course
- Split family into teams
- Each person does full course
- Next goes when previous finishes
- Team time matters!

### Build-Your-Own Course
- Each family member designs 2 stations
- Combine into mega course
- Designer demonstrates their stations
- Creativity rewarded!

## Age-Appropriate Modifications

### Little Ninjas (Ages 3-5)
- Fewer stations (5-6)
- Wider stepping stones
- Lower climbs
- No time pressure
- Parents assist as needed

### Junior Warriors (Ages 6-9)
- Standard 10 stations
- Moderate difficulty

### Teen Ninjas (Ages 10+)
- Advanced obstacles
- Strict timing
- Add penalty elements
- Competition mode!

### Adult Modifications
- Add weight carries
- Lower crawl spaces
- Higher jumps required
- Speed emphasis

## Competition Rules

### Individual Time Trial

**Rules:**
1. One person at a time
2. Timer starts on "GO"
3. Must complete each obstacle
4. Failed obstacle = 5-second penalty
5. Timer stops at finish line

**Scoring:**
- Best time wins
- Track personal bests
- Weekly improvement = victory!

### Head-to-Head Battles

**Setup:**
1. Two identical courses side by side
2. Two competitors start together
3. First to finish wins
4. Best of 3 matches

### Team Challenges

**Rules:**
1. Teams of 2-3 members
2. All must finish
3. Combined time = team score
4. Lowest combined time wins

## Training Progressions

### Week 1: Learn the Course
- Walk through each obstacle
- Practice difficult sections

### Week 2: First Time Trials

- Practice weak areas

### Week 3: Skill Work
- Dedicated obstacle practice
- Technique improvements
- Specific training

### Week 4: Competition Week
- Beat previous times
- Family tournament
- Crown the champion!

## Safety Guidelines

### Setup Safety
1. Test every obstacle before competing
2. Secure unstable items
3. Clear sharp edges
4. Padding at landing zones
5. Non-slip surfaces

### Competition Safety
1. Warm up first!
2. Demonstrate proper technique
3. Spot each other when needed
4. Stop if injured
5. Hydrate between attempts

### Environment Safety
1. Check weather for outdoor
2. Good lighting for indoor
3. Pets secured away
4. Enough space between obstacles
5. Clear emergency path
