## Summer Safety

### Sun Safety
- Sunscreen every 80 minutes
- Hats and protective clothing
- Avoid peak sun (10am-4pm)
- Shade breaks
- UV-protective sunglasses

### Heat Safety
- Hydrate constantly
- Early morning or evening exercise
- Watch for heat exhaustion
- Rest in shade
- Know the signs of heat stroke

### Water Safety
- Swim with a buddy
- Lifeguard on duty when possible
- Know your limits
- Life jackets for non-swimmers
- Adult supervision always

### Bug Safety
- Insect repellent
- Check for ticks after hikes
- Avoid dawn/dusk peak mosquito times
- Wear light colors

## Tips for Success

### Beat the Heat
- Early morning activities
- Water activities midday
- Evening sports

### Keep It Fun
- Let kids choose activities
- No forced participation
- Celebrate all efforts
- Ice cream helps!

### Stay Flexible
- Weather changes plans
- Energy varies
- Some days are rest days
- Progress isn't linear

### Include Everyone
- Mixed-age activities
- Various ability levels
- Everyone contributes ideas
- No one left out
