## Your Week to Transform!

Seven days. Fifteen minutes daily. One family mission: Move together and have fun doing it!

This challenge is designed for families new to working out together or looking to restart their fitness journey. By the end of the week, you'll have built momentum, discovered activities you love, and proven that family fitness is achievable.

---

## How This Challenge Works

### The Rules
1. **15 minutes minimum** each day (more is great!)
2. **Everyone participates** at their level
3. **Check in** at the end of each workout
4. **No makeup days**‚Äîmiss one? Just continue with the next
5. **Celebrate** completing each day!

### What You'll Need
- Space to move (living room works!)
- Comfortable clothes
- Water
- Great attitudes

## Day 1: Dance Party Warm-Up üíÉ

**Duration**: 15 minutes
**Vibe**: High energy, silly fun

### The Workout
1. **3 min**: Free dance warm-up (put on favorite songs)
2. **4 min**: Freeze dance (music stops = freeze!)
3. **4 min**: Dance style challenge:
   - 1 min: Dance like robots
   - 1 min: Dance like you're in slow motion
   - 1 min: Dance using only your upper body
   - 1 min: Dance as BIG as possible
4. **4 min**: Cool-down with slow song and stretching

### Family Check-In
- Favorite moment?
- How do you feel?
- Ready for Day 2?

---

## Day 2: Animal Adventure ü¶Å

**Duration**: 15 minutes
**Vibe**: Playful, imaginative

### The Workout
1. **2 min**: Wake up stretches
2. **10 min**: Animal circuit (45 sec each, 15 sec rest):
   - Bear walks
   - Frog jumps
   - Crab walks
   - Kangaroo hops
   - Snake slithers (army crawl)
   - Flamingo balance
   - Gorilla shuffle
   - Cheetah run (high knees)
3. **3 min**: Animal yoga poses (cat/cow, downward dog, cobra)

### Family Check-In
- Which animal was hardest?
- Which was funniest?
- What animal should we add tomorrow?

---

## Day 3: Strength Stars ‚≠ê

**Duration**: 15 minutes
**Vibe**: Empowering, challenging

### The Workout
1. **2 min**: Marching warm-up
2. **10 min**: Strength circuit (3 rounds):
   - 10 squats
   - 10 arm circles (each direction)
   - 5 push-ups (knees down OK!)
   - 20-second plank
   - 10 lunges (total)
   - Rest 30 seconds between rounds
3. **3 min**: Stretching

### Family Check-In
- What's stronger than yesterday?
- Favorite exercise?
- Toughest exercise?

---

## Day 4: Obstacle Course Challenge üèÉ

**Duration**: 15 minutes
**Vibe**: Creative, adventurous

### The Workout
1. **5 min**: Build course together using:
   - Pillows (stepping stones)
   - Chair (crawl under)
   - Line on floor (balance beam)
   - Laundry basket (ball toss)
   - Anything else you have!
2. **8 min**: Everyone completes course multiple times
   - Try for personal best times
   - Or complete together as a team
3. **2 min**: Stretching

### Family Check-In
- Best obstacle?
- Time to beat for next time?
- What should we add?

---

## Day 5: Yoga Journey üßò

**Duration**: 15 minutes
**Vibe**: Calm, connected

### The Workout
1. **2 min**: Breathing together (4 counts in, 4 counts out)
2. **10 min**: Family flow:
   - Mountain pose (30 sec)
   - Sun breath x 4
   - Warrior I (30 sec each side)
   - Warrior II (30 sec each side)
   - Tree pose (30 sec each side)
   - Forward fold (30 sec)
   - Cat/cow flows (1 min)
   - Child's pose (30 sec)
3. **3 min**: Relaxation‚Äîlie still, breathe, rest

### Family Check-In
- Feeling relaxed?
- Favorite pose?
- Could we do this before bed?

---

## Day 6: HIIT It! üî•

**Duration**: 15 minutes
**Vibe**: Energetic, sweaty

### The Workout
1. **2 min**: Warm-up jog in place
2. **10 min**: HIIT circuit (20 sec work, 10 sec rest):
   - Jumping jacks
   - Rest
   - High knees
   - Rest
   - Mountain climbers
   - Rest
   - Burpees (or squat jumps)
   - Rest
   - Repeat 2-3 rounds!
3. **3 min**: Cool-down walk and stretch

### Family Check-In
- How hard did you work (1-10)?
- Most challenging moment?
- Proud of pushing through?

---

## Day 7: Celebration Workout üéâ

**Duration**: 20 minutes (bonus day!)
**Vibe**: Joyful, reflective

### The Workout
1. **5 min**: Family's choice‚Äîvote on favorite from the week!

   - Everyone does each person's move

4. **5 min**: Victory dance party + stretching

### Family Check-In
- What did we accomplish this week?
- Favorite day?
- What do we want to continue?
