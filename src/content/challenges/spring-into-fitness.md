# Spring Into Fitness Family Challenge

Winter hibernation is OVER! Time to emerge, stretch, and spring into action with 4 weeks of energizing family fitness.

## The Challenge

**Duration:** 4 weeks (28 days)
**Daily Goal:** 20-30 minutes of movement
**Weekly Goal:** 5 active days + 2 rest/play days
**Spring Goal:** Get OUTSIDE as much as possible!

## Why Spring Fitness?

Spring brings opportunity:
- Longer days = more daylight hours
- Warming weather = outdoor possibilities
- Nature awakening = explore together
- Energy boost = shake off winter sluggishness

This challenge helps your family BLOOM!

## Week 1: Wake Up & Warm Up

*Theme: Shaking Off the Winter*

### Daily Missions

**Day 1: Family Spring Walk**
- 20-minute neighborhood walk
- Notice signs of spring
- Count flowers, birds, green buds
- Get reacquainted with outside!

**Day 2: Spring Cleaning Workout**
- Clean windows (arm workout!)
- Organize garage (lifting!)
- Yard pickup (squat and lift!)
- Make cleaning active

**Day 3: Indoor-Outdoor Mix**
- 10 minutes indoor warm-up
- 15 minutes outdoor play
- Transition season style!

**Day 4: Garden Games**
- If you garden: planting workout
- No garden: park play
- Dig, squat, reach, carry

**Day 5: Bike/Scooter Day**
- Family bike ride
- Or scooters, rollerblades
- Explore a new path!

**Day 6: Active REST**
- Gentle family yoga
- Outside if weather permits

**Day 7: Play Day**
- Unstructured outdoor play
- Whatever sounds fun!

**Day 13: Active REST**

### Rainy Day Options

## Spring Safety Tips

1. **Sunscreen**: Start wearing it now!
2. **Hydration**: Warm weather = more water
3. **Bug protection**: As bugs emerge
4. **Allergy awareness**: Know triggers
5. **Gradual increase**: Don't overdo after winter
6. **Weather check**: Dress in layers
