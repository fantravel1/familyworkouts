## Tips for Success

### Beat the Heat
- Early morning or evening activities
- Water breaks every 15-20 minutes
- Sunscreen always!
- Find shaded routes and spots

### Rainy Day Alternatives
- Mall walking
- Indoor rock climbing
- Swimming (indoor pool)
- Active video games (counts for half points!)
- Garage/basement obstacle course

### Keep Momentum
- Post the chart where everyone sees it
- Check off activities together
- Celebrate weekly point totals
- Take lots of photos!
