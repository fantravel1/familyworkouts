## The Earning System

### Activity Minutes = Screen Minutes

**1:1 Ratio (Standard)**
- 20 minutes of exercise = 20 minutes of screens
- Fair and simple!

**2:1 Ratio (More Activity)**
- 20 minutes of exercise = 40 minutes of screens
- Rewards effort generously

**1:2 Ratio (Challenging)**
- 20 minutes of exercise = 10 minutes of screens
- For families wanting significant change

*Choose what works for your family!*

## Handling Challenges

### "But I already earned my time!"
- Set daily caps regardless of earning
- Balance still matters
- Quality over quantity

### "Exercise is boring!"
- Let them choose the activity
- Make it social
- Try something new
- Gamify it!

### "My friend doesn't have to do this!"
- Different families, different rules
- Focus on the benefits you're seeing
- Make it positive, not punishing

### "I forgot to earn!"
- No retroactive earning
- Tomorrow is a new day
- Consistency matters more than perfection

### "Can I bank for tomorrow?"
- You decide! Some families allow, some don't
- Weekend banking can work well
- Daily reset keeps it fresh
