# Winter Wellness Family Challenge

Don't let the cold weather freeze your fitness! This 6-week challenge keeps your family moving, bonding, and beating the winter blues‚Äîall from the comfort of home.

## The Challenge

**Duration:** 6 weeks (42 days)
**Daily Goal:** At least 15 minutes of movement
**Weekly Goal:** 5 active days + 2 rest days
**Family Goal:** Complete together as a team!

## Why Winter Wellness?

Winter brings challenges:
- Shorter days = less motivation
- Cold weather = less outdoor time
- Holiday treats = need for balance
- Seasonal moods = movement helps!

This challenge combats ALL of these with fun family fitness!

## Tips for Success

### Making It Stick

1. **Same time daily**: Build the habit
2. **Prep workout clothes**: Remove barriers
3. **Visual tracker**: Hang somewhere visible
4. **Accountability partner**: Keep each other going
5. **Grace for missed days**: Life happens, keep going!

### Dealing with Setbacks

- Missed a day? Do 10 minutes the next day, no guilt
- Feeling unmotivated? Do just 5 minutes
- Someone sick? Modified/gentle movement counts
- Holiday chaos? Active family games count!

### Keeping It Fun

- Change up the music
- Workout in different rooms
- Invite friends virtually
- Costumes/themes for workouts
- Surprise workout choices
